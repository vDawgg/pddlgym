## Domain description

A robot is tasked with preparing a movie and a specific amount of snacks.

The available actions are:
- rewind-movie-2
    - Rewinds the movie if it is at the two hour mark.
- rewind-movie
    - Rewinds the movie at any other time than the two hour mark.
- reset-counter
    - Resets the counter.
- get-chips
    - Gets the chips
- get-dip
    - Gets the dip
- get-pop
    - Gets the pop
- get-cheese
    - Gets the cheese
- get-crackers
    - Gets the crackers
