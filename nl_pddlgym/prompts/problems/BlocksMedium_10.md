## Problem description

There are 16 given blocks (b0 - b15). There are 4 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b8-b9-b10, b11-b12-b13-b14-b15, b0-b1-b2, b3-b4-b5-b6-b7. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b0-b10-b4-b2-b1, b5-b14-b15-b13-b12, b11-b9-b7.
