## Problem description

There are 2 satellites (satellite0, satellite1), 3 instruments (instrument0, instrument1, instrument2), 3 different image modes (infrared0, infrared1 and thermograph2) and 10 directions (GroundStation1, Phenomenon8 - Phenomenon9, Planet3, Planet5 and Star0, Star2, Star4, Star6, Star7). The satellites satellite0 and satellite1 have power available. The satellite satellite0 carries the instrument instrument0. The satellite satellite1 carries the instruments instrument1 and instrument2. The instrument instrument0 supports thermograph2 and infrared0. The instrument instrument0 is currently calibrated for the direction Star0. The instrument instrument1 supports infrared0, thermograph2 and infrared1. The instrument instrument1 is currently calibrated for the direction Star2. The instrument instrument2 supports thermograph2 and infrared1. The instrument instrument2 is currently calibrated for the direction Star2. The satellite satellite0 is currently pointing in the direction of Star6 and satellite1 is currently pointing in the direction of Star0. The goal is for satellite1 to point to Planet5, to capture an image of Phenomenon8 with thermograph2, to capture an image of Phenomenon9 with infrared0, to capture an image of Planet3 with infrared1, to capture an image of Planet5 with thermograph2, to capture an image of Star4 with infrared1, to capture an image of Star6 with infrared1 and to capture an image of Star7 with infrared0.
