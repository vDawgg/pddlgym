## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-2-2 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-2. There is one bear (bear-0), one monkey (monkey-1) and one pawn (pawn-2). The bear bear-0 starts out at location loc-2-2. The monkey monkey-1 starts out at location loc-1-1. The pawn pawn-2 starts out at location loc-2-0. The goal is for the robot to hold bear-0 and for pawn-2 to be at loc-1-2.
