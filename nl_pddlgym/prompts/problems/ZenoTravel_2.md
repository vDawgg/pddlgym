## Problem description

There are two planes (plane1, plane2), four passengers (person1, person2, person3, person4), three cities (city0, city1, city2) and 7 possible fuel levels (fl0 - fl6). Plane1 starts out in city0 with fuel level fl4 and plane2 starts out in city2 with fuel level fl5. Passenger person1 is in city0, person2 is in city0, person3 is in city1 and person4 is in city1. The goal is that plane2 is in city2, person1 is in city1, person2 is in city0, person3 is in city0 and person4 is in city1.
