The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l1, c1 to l2, c2 to l3, c3 to l4, c4 to l5, c5 to l6, c6 to l1, c7 to l2, c8 to l3, c9 to l4, c10 to l5, c11 to l6, c12 to l1, c13 to l2 and c14 to l3.
