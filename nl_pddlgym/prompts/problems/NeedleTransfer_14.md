## Problem description

There is one needle (at location called: needle) lying on the ground in proximity of the right_arm and one goal position (at location called: goal) close to the left_arm. Additionally, there are 13 rings (at locations called: red_ring, green_ring, blue_ring, yellow_ring, orange_ring, purple_ring, pink_ring, cyan_ring, magenta_ring, teal_ring, brown_ring, gray_ring and olive_ring) in the middle of the scene.

The goal is for the robot to transfer the needle to the goal after passing the needle through the rings in the following sequence:
1. red_ring
2. green_ring
3. blue_ring
4. yellow_ring
5. orange_ring
6. purple_ring
7. pink_ring
8. cyan_ring
9. magenta_ring
10. teal_ring
11. brown_ring
12. gray_ring
13. olive_ring
