## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), five cars (c0 - c4), and ten locations (l0 - l9). The ferry start out at location l5. The car c0 is at location l4, c1 at l8, c2 at l5, c3 at l5, and c4 at l6. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l9, c1 to l0, c2 to l7, c3 to l7 and c4 to l4.
