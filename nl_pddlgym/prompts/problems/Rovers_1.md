## Problem description

There is one rover (rover0), one moon-lander (general), one rover storage (rover0store), two cameras (camera0, camera1), two objectives (objective0, objective1), three camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and four locations (waypoint0 - waypoint3). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint0. The lander general starts out at location waypoint1. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera0 and camera1. Camera0 can capture colour, high_res and low_res modes. Camera1 can capture high_res mode. Camera0 has the calibration target objective0 and camera1 has objective1. The rover rover0 is equipped for rock and soil analysis. waypoint0 contains rock and soil samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint0, rock data from waypoint0 and a low_res image of objective1.
