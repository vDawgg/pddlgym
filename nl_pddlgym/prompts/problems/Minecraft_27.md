## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-2), 2 logs (log-0, log-1), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-2 is at loc-1-0, log-0 at loc-4-0, log-1 at loc-1-1 and the agent is at loc-2-0. The goal is for the agent to carry grass-2 in its inventory and have new-2 equipped.
