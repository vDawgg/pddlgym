## Problem description

There are 6 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1, grass-2, grass-3, grass-4, grass-5), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-0 is at loc-4-0, grass-1 at loc-3-1, grass-2 at loc-3-2, grass-3 at loc-4-1, grass-4 at loc-3-1, grass-5 at loc-4-1 and the agent is at loc-4-0. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-3-0 and carry new-2 in its inventory.
