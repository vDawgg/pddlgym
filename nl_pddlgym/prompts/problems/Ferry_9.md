## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), 10 cars (c0 - c9), and 10 locations (l0 - l9). The ferry start out at location l0. The car c0 is at location l0, c1 at l1, c2 at l2, c3 at l3, c4 at l4, c5 at l5, c6 at l6, c7 at l7, c8 at l8 and c9 at l9. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l1, c1 to l2, c2 to l3, c3 to l4, c4 to l5, c5 to l6, c6 to l7, c7 to l8, c8 to l9 and c9 to l1.
