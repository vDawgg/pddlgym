## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-4-3 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-3. There is one bear (bear-0), one pawn (pawn-1) and one monkey (monkey-2). The bear bear-0 starts out at location loc-2-2. The pawn pawn-1 starts out at location loc-1-2. The monkey monkey-2 starts out at location loc-4-3. The goal is to move bear-0 to loc-0-2.
