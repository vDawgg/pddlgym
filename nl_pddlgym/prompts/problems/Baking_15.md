## Problem description

There are 2 ovens (oven-0, oven-1), 3 eggs (egg-0, egg-1, egg-2), 3 bags of flour (flour-0, flour-1, flour-2), 2 bars of soap (soap-0, soap-1), 1 clean pan (pan-0), and 3 cakes that should be baked (new-0, new-1 and new-2). The goal is to bake the cakes new-0, new-1 and new-2.
