## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1), 2 logs (log-2, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-4. grass-0 is at loc-1-3, grass-1 at loc-4-2, log-2 at loc-0-0, log-3 at loc-4-3 and the agent is at loc-2-3. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-0-4.
