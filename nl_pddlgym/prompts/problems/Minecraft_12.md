## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-0), 3 logs (log-1, log-2, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-4. grass-0 is at loc-0-3, log-1 at loc-2-3, log-2 at loc-0-0, log-3 at loc-0-0 and the agent is at loc-1-3. The goal is for the agent to having crafted a plank in new-1.
