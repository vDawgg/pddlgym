## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-1), 2 logs (log-0, log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-4. grass-1 is at loc-2-0, log-0 at loc-1-3, log-2 at loc-1-0 and the agent is at loc-1-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-0-3.
