## Problem description

There are fifteen locations (loc-0 - loc-14) and twenty-four newspapers (paper-0 - paper-23). The robot starts out at loc-0. All newspapers are located at loc-0. The following locations needs newspapers delivered: loc-1, loc-2, loc-3, loc-4, loc-5, loc-6, loc-7, loc-8, loc-9, loc-10, loc-11, loc-12, loc-13 and loc-14.
