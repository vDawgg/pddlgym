## Problem Description

There are 3 floors (f0, f1, f2) and 2 passengers (p0, p1). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0, f1, f2. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f0 and p1 is at f1. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f1 and p1 to f2.
