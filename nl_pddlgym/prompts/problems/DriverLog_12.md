## Problem description

There are two robots (driver1, driver2), three trucks (truck1, truck2, truck3), six packages (package1 - package6) and twelve locations (s0 - s11). The driver driver1 starts out at location s0 and driver2 at s9. The truck truck1 starts out at location s3, truck2 at s9 and truck3 at s2. The package package1 starts out at location s4, package2 at s6, package3 at s5, package4 at s6, package5 at s0 and package6 at s2. The goal is for driver1 to be at s11, truck2 at s8, truck3 at s4, package1 at s5, package2 at s0, package3 at s4, package4 at s0 and package6 at s5.
