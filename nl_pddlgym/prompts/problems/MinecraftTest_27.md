## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-2), 3 logs (log-1, log-3, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-2. grass-0 is at loc-2-1, grass-2 at loc-1-2, log-1 at loc-3-2, log-3 at loc-2-0, log-4 at loc-2-1 and the agent is at loc-2-1. The goal is for the agent to have crafted a plank in new-2.
