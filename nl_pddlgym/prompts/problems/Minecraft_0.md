## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1), 1 log (log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-3. grass-0 is at loc-2-2, grass-1 at loc-0-0, log-2 at loc-3-0 and the agent is at loc-0-2. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-1-1 and carry grass-1 in its inventory.
