## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-2-2 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-0. There are three pawns (pawn-0 - pawn-2) and one monkey (monkey-3). The pawn pawn-0 starts out at location loc-0-0. The pawn pawn-1 starts out at location loc-1-2. The pawn pawn-2 starts out at location loc-0-2. The monkey monkey-3 starts out at location loc-1-1. The goal is to move pawn-0 to loc-2-1.
