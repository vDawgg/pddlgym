## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-0), 2 logs (log-1, log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-3. grass-0 is at loc-2-0, log-1 at loc-2-0, log-2 at loc-1-1 and the agent is at loc-0-0. The goal is for the agent to carry new-2 in its inventory.
