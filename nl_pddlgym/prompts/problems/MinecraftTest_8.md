## Problem description

There are 4 grass-items (grass-0, grass-2, grass-3, grass-4), 2 logs (log-1, log-5), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-3. grass-0 is at loc-2-1, grass-2 at loc-0-2, grass-3 at loc-0-3, grass-4 at loc-1-0, log-1 at loc-1-0, log-5 at loc-0-1 and the agent is at loc-1-3. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-2-1 and have crafted a plank in new-1.
