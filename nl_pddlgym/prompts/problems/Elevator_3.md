## Problem Description

There are ten floors (f0 - f9) and five passengers (p0 - p4). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0,f1,f2,f3,f4,f5,f6,f7,f8,f9. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f3, p1 is at f7, p2 is at f3, p3 is at f6 and p4 is at f9. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f3, p1 to f7, p2 to f3, p3 to f6 and p4 is at f9.
