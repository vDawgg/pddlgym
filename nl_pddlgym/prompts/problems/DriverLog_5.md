## Problem description

There are three robots (driver1, driver2, driver3), three trucks (truck1, truck2, truck3), five packages (package1 - package5) and three locations (s0 - s2). The driver driver1 starts out at location s2, driver2 at s2 and driver3 at s1. The truck truck1 starts out at location s0, truck2 at s1 and truck3 at s1. The package package1 starts out at location s1, package2 at s1, package3 at s0, package4 at s1 and package5 at s1. The goal is for driver1 to be at s1, driver2 at s0, truck1 at s0, truck2 at s0, truck3 at s2, package1 at s2, package2 at s1, package3 at s0, package4 at s1 and package5 at s1.
