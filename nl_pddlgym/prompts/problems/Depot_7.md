## Problem description

There is 1 depot (depot0), 2 distributors (distibutor0, distributor1), 2 trucks (truck0, truck1), 6 pallets (palet0 - pallet5), 10 crates (crate0 - crate9) and 3 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist2). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1, pallet4 and pallet5 at distributor0, pallet2 and pallet3 at distributor1. crate0 is on pallet4, crate1 on pallet1, crate2 on pallet0, crate3 is on pallet5, crate4 on pallet3, crate5 on crate1, crate6 on crate5, crate7 on crate4, crate8 on crate3 and crate9 on pallet2. truck0 and truck1 are at distributor0. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at distributor0 and hoist2 at distributor1. The goal is to transfer crate0 on pallet3, crate1 on crate0, crate3 on crate8, crate6 on pallet2, crate7 on pallet1, crate8 on pallet4 and crate9 on pallet0.
