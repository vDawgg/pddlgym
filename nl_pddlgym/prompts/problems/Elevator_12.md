## Problem Description

There are 10 floors (f0 - f9) and 9 passengers (p0 - p8). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0, f1, f2, f3, f4, f5, f6, f7, f8, f9. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f0, p1 is at f1, p2 is at f2, p3 is at f3, p4 is at f4, p5 is at f5, p6 is at f6, p7 is at f7 and p8 is at f8. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f1, p1 to f2, p2 to f3, p3 to f4, p4 to f5, p5 to f6, p6 to f7, p7 to f8 and p8 to f9.
