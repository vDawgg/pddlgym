## Problem description

There are 2 rovers (rover0, rover1), one lander (general), 2 rover storages (rover0store, rover1store), 3 cameras (camera0, camera1, camera2), 3 objectives (objective0, objective1, objective2), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 4 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint3). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint0 and rover1 starts out at location waypoint0. The lander general starts out at location waypoint3. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The rover rover0 is equipped for rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera0 and camera1. Camera camera0 can capture high_res and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective1 and camera camera1 can capture colour and high_res mode and has the calibration target objective1. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera2 that can capture the following modes: colour, high_res and low_res. The camera camera2 has the calibration target objective1. waypoint1 contains soil and rock samples and waypoint2 and waypoint3 contain soil samples and waypoint0 contains rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint1, soil data from waypoint2, rock data from waypoint0, rock data from waypoint1, a colour image of objective0, a high_res image of objective0 and a high_res image of objective2.
