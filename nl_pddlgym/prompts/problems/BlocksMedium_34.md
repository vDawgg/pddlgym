## Problem description

There are 29 given blocks (b0 - b28). There are 6 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b10-b11-b12-b13-b14, b15-b16-b17-b18-b19, b20-b21-b22-b23, b24-b25-b26-b27-b28, b0-b1-b2-b3-b4, b5-b6-b7-b8-b9. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b26-b13-b27-b14-b11, b8-b18-b23-b12, b0-b6-b4-b15, b20-b3-b16-b7-b25.
