There are 4 depots (depot0 - depot3), 4 distributors (distibutor0 - distributor3), 4 trucks (truck0 - truck3), 10 pallets (palet0 - pallet9), 8 crates (crate0 - crate7) and 8 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist7). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1 at depot1, pallet2 at depot2, pallet3 and pallet9 at depot3, pallet4 at distributor0, pallet5 at distributor1, and pallet6 and pallet8 at distributor2, and pallet7 at distributor3. crate0 is on pallet9, crate1 is on pallet1, crate2 is on pallet4, crate3 is on pallet5 with crate5 stacked on top, crate4 is on pallet8, crate6 is on pallet0 and crate7 is on pallet3. truck0 is at depot0, truck1 at distributor0, truck2 at depot2 and truck3 at distributor3. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at depot1, hoist2 at depot2, hoist3 at depot3, hoist4 at distributor0, hoist5 at distributor1, hoist6 at distributor2 and hoist7 at distributor3. The goal is to transfer crate0 on pallet6, crate1 on pallet8, crate3 on crate1, crate4 on pallet5, crate5 on crate7, crate6 on pallet4 and crate7 on crate4.
