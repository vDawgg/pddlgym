## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), 8 cars (c0 - c7), and 5 locations (l0 - l4). The ferry start out at location l0. The car c0 is at location l0, c1 at l1, c2 at l2, c3 at l3, c4 at l4, c5 at l0, c6 at l1 and c7 at l2. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l1, c1 to l2, c2 to l3, c3 to l4, c4 to l1, c5 to l2, c6 to l3 and c7 to l4.
