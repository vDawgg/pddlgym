## Problem description

There are 4 rovers (rover0 - rover3), one lander (general), 4 rover storages (rover0store - rover3store), 6 cameras (camera0 - camera5), 4 objectives (objective0 - objective3), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 7 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint6). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint4, rover1 starts out at location waypoint0, rover2 starts out at location waypoint3 and rover3 starts out at location waypoint1. The lander general starts out at location waypoint1. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The storage rover2store is empty and attached to rover2. The storage rover3store is empty and attached to rover3. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil and rock analysis. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover2 is equipped for rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera0 and camera1 and camera2 and camera3. Camera camera0 can capture low_res mode and has the calibration target objective2, camera camera1 can capture colour mode and has the calibration target objective3, camera camera2 can capture colour and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective1 and camera camera3 can capture high_res and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective2. The rover rover2 is equipped with the camera camera4 that can capture the following modes: colour. The camera camera4 has the calibration target objective0. The rover rover3 is equipped with the camera camera5 that can capture the following modes: colour, high_res and low_res. The camera camera5 has the calibration target objective0. waypoint0, waypoint3, waypoint4 and waypoint6 contain soil and rock samples and waypoint1 contains rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint0, soil data from waypoint3, soil data from waypoint4, soil data from waypoint6, rock data from waypoint0, rock data from waypoint1, rock data from waypoint3, rock data from waypoint4, a colour image of objective2, a colour image of objective3 and a low_res image of objective3.
