## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-2-2 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-1-1. There are two monkeys (monkey-0, monkey-1) and two pawns (pawn-2, pawn-3). The monkey monkey-0 starts out at location loc-0-1. The monkey monkey-1 starts out at location loc-2-0. The pawn pawn-2 starts out at location loc-2-0. The pawn pawn-3 starts out at location loc-1-2. The goal is to move both pawn-3 and monkey-1 to loc-1-1.
