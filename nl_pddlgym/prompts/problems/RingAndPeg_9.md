## Problem description

There are 6 colored pegs with the following colors: brown, cyan, gray, magenta, olive and teal. The pegs are all named in the format <color>_peg. Additionally there are 3 colored rings: cyan, magenta and teal. Similarly to the pegs, the rings are named <color>_ring.

The robot arm starts out in a default position, while the rings start in the following positions:
- cyan_ring - teal_peg
- magenta_ring - magenta_peg
- teal_ring - olive_peg

The goal is to transfer all rings to the pegs of their color.
