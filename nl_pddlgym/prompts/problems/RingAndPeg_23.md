## Problem description

There are 9 colored pegs with the following colors: blue, coral, gold, green, indigo, pink, red, silver and yellow. The pegs are all named in the format <color>_peg. Additionally there are 8 colored rings: blue, coral, gold, green, indigo, pink, red and silver. Similarly to the pegs, the rings are named <color>_ring.

The robot arm starts out in a default position, while the rings start in the following positions:
- coral_ring - coral_peg
- gold_ring - green_peg
- silver_ring - blue_peg
- indigo_ring - pink_peg
- red_ring - gold_peg
- green_ring - silver_peg
- blue_ring - indigo_peg
- pink_ring - red_peg

The goal is to transfer all rings to the pegs of their color.
