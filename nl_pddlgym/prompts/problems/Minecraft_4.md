## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-2), 3 logs (log-1, log-3, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-0 is at loc-3-2, grass-2 at loc-3-2, log-1 at loc-2-0, log-3 at loc-0-2, log-4 loc-0-2 and the agent is at loc-3-0. The goal is for the agent to carry new-0 in its inventory.
