## Problem description

There are 5 pegs (peg1 - peg5) and 5 rings (d1 - d5). The rings have the following order according to their size (largest to smallest): d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. peg2, peg3, peg4 and peg5 have no rings stacked on them. peg1 has rings stacked on it in the following order (bottom to top): d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. The rings should be stacked from largest to smallest on peg5.
