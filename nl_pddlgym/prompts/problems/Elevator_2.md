## Problem Description

There are eight floors (f0 - f7) and four passengers (p0 - p3). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0,f1,f2,f3,f4,f5,f6,f7. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f7, p1 is at f1, p2 is at f1 and p3 is at f2. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f6, p1 to f3, p2 to f7 and p3 to f4.
