## Problem description

There are 3 grass-items (grass-0, grass-2, grass-3), 2 logs (log-1, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-0 is at loc-0-0, grass-2 at loc-0-1, grass-3 at loc-0-0, log-1 at loc-0-2, log-4 at loc-1-1 and the agent is at loc-2-0. The goal is for the agent to carry new-0 in its inventory.
