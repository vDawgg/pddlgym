## Problem description

There are six locations (l0 - l5) and five items (o0 - o4). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l1. The item o0 starts out at location l2, o1 at l0, o2 at l3, o3 at l4 and o4 at l0. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l0, the item o0 to be at location l5, o1 at l1, o2 at l1, o3 at l5 and o4 at l5.
