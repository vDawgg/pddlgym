## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), 6 cars (c0 - c5), and 4 locations (l0 - l3). The ferry start out at location l0. The car c0 is at location l0, c1 at l1, c2 at l2, c3 at l3, c4 at l0 and c5 at l1. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l1, c1 to l2, c2 to l3, c3 to l1, c4 to l2 and c5 to l3.
