## Problem Description

There are 11 floors (f0 - f10) and 10 passengers (p0 - p9). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0, f1, f2, f3, f4, f5, f6, f7, f8, f9, f10. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f0, p1 is at f1, p2 is at f2, p3 is at f3, p4 is at f4, p5 is at f5, p6 is at f6, p7 is at f7, p8 is at f8 and p9 is at f9. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f1, p1 to f2, p2 to f3, p3 to f4, p4 to f5, p5 to f6, p6 to f7, p7 to f8, p8 to f9 and p9 to f10.
