## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), six cars (c0 - c5), and ten locations (l0 - l9). The ferry start out at location l2. The car c0 is at location l7, c1 at l6, c2 at l1, c3 at l9, c4 at l0, and c5 at l1. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l0, c1 to l9, c2 to l6, c3 to l9, c4 to l4 and c5 to l8.
