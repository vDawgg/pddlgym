## Problem description

There are 3 rovers (rover0, rover1, rover2), one lander (general), 3 rover storages (rover0store, rover1store, rover2store), 2 cameras (camera0, camera1), 2 objectives (objective0, objective1), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 6 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint5). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint2, rover1 starts out at location waypoint3 and rover2 starts out at location waypoint4. The lander general starts out at location waypoint3. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The storage rover2store is empty and attached to rover2. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for rock analysis. The rover rover2 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera0 that can capture the following modes: colour and high_res. The camera camera0 has the calibration target objective0. The rover rover2 is equipped with the camera camera1 that can capture the following modes: high_res. The camera camera1 has the calibration target objective1. waypoint4 contains soil and rock samples and waypoint1 contains soil samples and waypoint2, waypoint3 and waypoint5 contain rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint1, soil data from waypoint4, rock data from waypoint2, rock data from waypoint3, rock data from waypoint4 and a high_res image of objective0.
