## Problem description

There are two robots (driver1, driver2), two trucks (truck1, truck2), three packages (package1 - package3) and three locations (s0 - s2). The driver driver1 starts out at location s0 and driver2 at s0. The truck truck1 starts out at location s0 and truck2 at s1. The package package1 starts out at location s2, package2 at s1 and package3 at s1. The goal is for driver1 to be at s1, driver2 at s1, truck1 at s2, truck2 at s0, package1 at s0 and package2 at s2 and package3 at s0.
