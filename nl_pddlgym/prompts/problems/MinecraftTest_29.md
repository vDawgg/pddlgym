## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-0), 3 logs (log-1, log-2, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-4. grass-0 is at loc-1-4, log-1 at loc-3-2, log-2 at loc-0-0, log-3 at loc-2-1 and the agent is at loc-1-1. The goal is for the agent to carry new-1 in its inventory.
