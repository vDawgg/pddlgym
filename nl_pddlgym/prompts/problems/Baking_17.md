## Problem description

There are 5 ovens (oven-0 - oven-4), 5 eggs (egg-0 - egg-4), 3 bags of flour (flour-0, flour-1, flour-2), 2 bars of soap (soap-0, soap-1), 3 clean pans (pan-0, pan-1, pan-2), and 3 cakes that should be baked (new-0, new-1 and new-2) and 2 souffles that should be baked (new-3 and new-4). The goal is to bake the cakes new-0, new-1 and new-2 and the souffles new-3 and new-4.
