## Problem description

There are 42 colored needles (the needle locations are called: blue_needle_1 - blue_needle_6, green_needle_1 - green_needle_6, orange_needle_1 - orange_needle_6, pink_needle_1 - pink_needle_6, purple_needle_1 - purple_needle_6, red_needle_1 - red_needle_6 and yellow_needle_1 - yellow_needle_6) and 7 goals (blue_goal, green_goal, orange_goal, pink_goal, purple_goal, red_goal and yellow_goal). The robot arm start out in a default position, while the needles are scattered randomly away from the goal positions. The goal is to move all needles to their matching goals.
