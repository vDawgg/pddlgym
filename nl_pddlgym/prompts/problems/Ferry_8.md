The car c0 is at location l0, c1 at l1, c2 at l2, c3 at l3, c4 at l4, c5 at l5, c6 at l6, c7 at l7, c8 at l0, c9 at l1, c10 at l2 and c11 at l3. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l1, c1 to l2, c2 to l3, c3 to l4, c4 to l5, c5 to l6, c6 to l7, c7 to l1, c8 to l2, c9 to l3, c10 to l4 and c11 to l5.
