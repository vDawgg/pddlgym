## Problem description

There are two robots (driver1, driver2), two trucks (truck1, truck2), four packages (package1 - package4) and three locations (s0 - s2). The driver driver1 starts out at location s1 and driver2 at s0. The truck truck1 starts out at location s1 and truck2 at s2. The package package1 starts out at location s0, package2 at s0, package3 at s1 and package4 at s1. The goal is for driver2 to be at s2, truck1 at s1, truck2 at s2, package1 at s1, package2 at s1 and package3 at s2.
