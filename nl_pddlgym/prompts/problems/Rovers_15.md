## Problem description

There are 4 rovers (rover0 - rover3), one lander (general), 4 rover storages (rover0store - rover3store), 4 cameras (camera0 - camera3), 5 objectives (objective0 - objective4), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 12 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint11). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint6, rover1 starts out at location waypoint2, rover2 starts out at location waypoint9 and rover3 starts out at location waypoint0. The lander general starts out at location waypoint6. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The storage rover2store is empty and attached to rover2. The storage rover3store is empty and attached to rover3. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil and rock analysis. The rover rover2 is equipped for rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped for rock analysis. The rover rover2 is equipped with the camera camera0 and camera1 and camera3. Camera camera0 can capture low_res mode and has the calibration target objective3, camera camera1 can capture colour mode and has the calibration target objective4 and camera camera3 can capture colour, high_res and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective3. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera2 that can capture the following modes: high_res. The camera camera2 has the calibration target objective2. waypoint1, waypoint5, waypoint8, waypoint9 and waypoint11 contain soil and rock samples and waypoint0, waypoint4 and waypoint7 contain soil samples and waypoint2, waypoint3 and waypoint10 contain rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint1, soil data from waypoint4, soil data from waypoint7, soil data from waypoint9, rock data from waypoint1, rock data from waypoint3, rock data from waypoint5, rock data from waypoint10, a high_res image of objective0, a high_res image of objective2 and a high_res image of objective4.
