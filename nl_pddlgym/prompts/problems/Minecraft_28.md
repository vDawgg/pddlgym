## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-1, grass-2), 2 logs (log-0, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-4. grass-1 is at loc-2-1, grass-2 at loc-0-0, log-0 at loc-0-2, log-3 at loc-0-2 and the agent is at loc-0-0. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-2-3.
