## Problem description

There is one needle (at location called: needle) lying on the ground in proximity of the right_arm and one goal position (at location called: goal) close to the left_arm. Additionally, there are 14 rings (at locations called: teal_ring, brown_ring, gray_ring, olive_ring, maroon_ring, navy_ring, lime_ring, coral_ring, gold_ring, silver_ring, indigo_ring, red_ring, green_ring and blue_ring) in the middle of the scene.

The goal is for the robot to transfer the needle to the goal after passing the needle through the rings in the following sequence:
1. teal_ring
2. brown_ring
3. gray_ring
4. olive_ring
5. maroon_ring
6. navy_ring
7. lime_ring
8. coral_ring
9. gold_ring
10. silver_ring
11. indigo_ring
12. red_ring
13. green_ring
14. blue_ring
