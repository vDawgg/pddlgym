## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-4), 3 logs (log-1, log-2, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-2. grass-0 is at loc-1-1, grass-4 at loc-0-0, log-1 at loc-1-0, log-2 at loc-2-2, log-3 at loc-3-1 and the agent is at loc-1-0. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-0-1.
