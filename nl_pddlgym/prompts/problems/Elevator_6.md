## Problem Description

There are 4 floors (f0 - f3) and 3 passengers (p0, p1, p2). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0, f1, f2, f3. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f0, p1 is at f1 and p2 is at f2. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f1, p1 to f2 and p2 to f3.
