## Problem description

There are 27 given blocks (b0 - b26). There are 6 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b9-b10-b11-b12, b13-b14-b15-b16-b17, b18-b19-b20-b21, b22-b23-b24-b25-b26, b0-b1-b2-b3, b4-b5-b6-b7-b8. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b23-b13-b5-b1, b3-b14-b22, b26-b11-b21-b15-b24.
