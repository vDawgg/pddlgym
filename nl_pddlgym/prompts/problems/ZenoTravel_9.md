## Problem description

There are three planes (plane1, plane2, plane3), eight passengers (person1, person2, person3, person4, person5, person6, person7, person8), five cities (city0, city1, city2, city3, city4) and 7 possible fuel levels (fl0 - fl6). Plane1 starts out in city0 with fuel level fl2, plane2 starts out in city4 with fuel level fl5 and plane3 starts out in city2 with fuel level fl2. Passenger person1 is in city3, person2 is in city3, person3 is in city4, person4 is in city4, person5 is in city1, person6 is in city0, person7 is in city1 and person8 is in city0. The goal is that plane1 is in city2, person1 is in city1, person2 is in city2, person3 is in city3, person4 is in city1, person5 is in city0, person6 is in city3, person7 is in city4 and person8 is in city3.
