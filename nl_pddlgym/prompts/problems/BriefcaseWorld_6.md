## Problem description

There are eight locations (l0 - l7) and seven items (o0 - o6). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l6. The item o0 starts out at location l0, o1 at l6, o2 at l5, o3 at l5, o4 at l7, o5 at l6 and o6 at l5. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l7, the item o0 to be at location l4, o1 at l3, o2 at l1, o3 at l3, o4 at l5, o5 at l2 and o6 at l6.
