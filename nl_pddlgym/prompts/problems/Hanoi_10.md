## Problem description

There are 6 pegs (peg1 - peg6) and 6 rings (d1 - d6). The rings have the following order according to their size (largest to smallest): d6, d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. peg2, peg3, peg4, peg5 and peg6 have no rings stacked on them. peg1 has rings stacked on it in the following order (bottom to top): d6, d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. The rings should be stacked from largest to smallest on peg6.
