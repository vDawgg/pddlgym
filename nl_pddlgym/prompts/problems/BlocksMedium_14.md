## Problem description

There are 15 given blocks (b0 - b14). There are 4 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b8-b9-b10-b11, b12-b13-b14, b0-b1-b2-b3, b4-b5-b6-b7. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b0-b4-b2-b14-b10, b13-b1-b3-b9-b11, b12, b6-b7-b8-b5.
