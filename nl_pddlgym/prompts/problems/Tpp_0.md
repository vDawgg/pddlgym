## Problem description

There is one type of goods (goods1), one truck (truck1), one market (market1), one depot (depot1), and two possible levels (level0, level1). The truck truck1 has no goods of type goods1 loaded. The depot has no goods of type goods1 stored. The market market1 has one level of goods of type goods1 on sale. No goods of type goods1 have been purchased yet at market1. The truck is currently at depot1. The goal is to store 1 level of goods of type goods1 at the depot.
