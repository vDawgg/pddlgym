## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-2-3 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-1. There is one monkey (monkey-0) and one bear (bear-1). The monkey monkey-0 starts out at location loc-0-2. The bear bear-1 starts out at location loc-1-2. The goal is for the robot to hold bear-1 and for bear-1 to be at location loc-2-2.
