## Problem description

There are 10 colored pegs with the following colors: blue, coral, gold, green, indigo, lime, navy, pink, red and silver. The pegs are all named in the format <color>_peg. Additionally there are 7 colored rings: coral, gold, indigo, lime, navy, red and silver. Similarly to the pegs, the rings are named <color>_ring.

The robot arm starts out in a default position, while the rings start in the following positions:
- navy_ring - navy_peg
- lime_ring - lime_peg
- coral_ring - green_peg
- gold_ring - blue_peg
- silver_ring - pink_peg
- indigo_ring - coral_peg
- red_ring - red_peg

The goal is to transfer all rings to the pegs of their color.
