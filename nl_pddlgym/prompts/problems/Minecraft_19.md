## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-3, grass-5), 4 logs (log-0, log-1, log-2, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-3. grass-3 is at loc-0-2, grass-5 at loc-1-1, log-0 at loc-2-3, log-1 at loc-1-1, log-2 at loc-1-3, log-4 at loc-1-0 and the agent is at loc-1-3. The goal is for the agent to carry log-4 in its inventory and have new-1 equipped.
