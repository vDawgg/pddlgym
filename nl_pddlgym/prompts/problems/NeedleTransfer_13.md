## Problem description

There is one needle (at location called: needle) lying on the ground in proximity of the right_arm and one goal position (at location called: goal) close to the left_arm. Additionally, there are 12 rings (at locations called: cyan_ring, magenta_ring, teal_ring, brown_ring, gray_ring, olive_ring, maroon_ring, navy_ring, lime_ring, coral_ring, gold_ring and silver_ring) in the middle of the scene.

The goal is for the robot to transfer the needle to the goal after passing the needle through the rings in the following sequence:
1. cyan_ring
2. magenta_ring
3. teal_ring
4. brown_ring
5. gray_ring
6. olive_ring
7. maroon_ring
8. navy_ring
9. lime_ring
10. coral_ring
11. gold_ring
12. silver_ring
