## Problem description

There are 1 satellites (satellite0), 2 instruments (instrument0, instrument1), 3 different image modes (image2, infrared0 and infrared1) and 8 directions (GroundStation1 - GroundStation2, Phenomenon5 - Phenomenon6, Planet3 - Planet4 and Star0, Star7). The satellite satellite0 has power available. The satellite satellite0 carries the instruments instrument0 and instrument1. The instrument instrument0 supports infrared1 and infrared0. The instrument instrument0 is currently calibrated for the direction Star0. The instrument instrument1 supports image2, infrared1 and infrared0. The instrument instrument1 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation2. The satellite satellite0 is currently pointing in the direction of Planet4. The goal is to capture an image of Phenomenon5 with image2, to capture an image of Phenomenon6 with infrared0, to capture an image of Planet3 with infrared0, to capture an image of Planet4 with infrared0 and to capture an image of Star7 with infrared0.
