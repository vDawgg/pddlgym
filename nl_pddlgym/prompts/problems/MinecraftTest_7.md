## Problem description

There are 3 grass-items (grass-1, grass-3, grass-4), 2 logs (log-0, log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-1 is at loc-1-2, grass-3 at loc-1-0, grass-4 at loc-3-0, log-0 at loc-3-1, log-2 at loc-3-0 and the agent is at loc-4-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-2-1 and carry new-0 in its inventory.
