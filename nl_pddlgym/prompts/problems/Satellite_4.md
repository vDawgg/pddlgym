## Problem description

There are 3 satellites (satellite0, satellite1, satellite2), 9 instruments (instrument0 - instrument8), 3 different image modes (image2, spectrograph1 and thermograph0) and 10 directions (GroundStation0 - GroundStation2, Phenomenon5, Phenomenon6, Phenomenon8, Planet9 and Star3, Star4, Star7). The satellite satellite1 carries the instruments instrument3, instrument4 and instrument5. The satellite satellite2 carries the instruments instrument6, instrument7 and instrument8. The instrument instrument0 supports image2, thermograph0 and spectrograph1. The instrument instrument0 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation2. The instrument instrument1 supports thermograph0, spectrograph1 and image2. The instrument instrument1 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation1. The instrument instrument2 supports image2. The instrument instrument2 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation0. The instrument instrument3 supports spectrograph1 and thermograph0. The instrument instrument3 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation0. The instrument instrument4 supports image2 and spectrograph1. The instrument instrument4 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation2. The instrument instrument5 supports image2, spectrograph1 and thermograph0. The instrument instrument5 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation1. The instrument instrument6 supports image2. The instrument instrument6 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation1. The instrument instrument7 supports image2 and thermograph0. The instrument instrument7 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation1. The instrument instrument8 supports spectrograph1, image2 and thermograph0. The instrument instrument8 is currently calibrated for the direction GroundStation0. The satellite satellite0 is currently pointing in the direction of Phenomenon8, satellite1 is currently pointing in the direction of GroundStation2 and satellite2 is currently pointing in the direction of Phenomenon5. The goal is for satellite0 to point to Phenomenon5, for satellite1 to point to GroundStation2, to capture an image of Phenomenon5 with image2, to capture an image of Phenomenon6 with image2, to capture an image of Phenomenon8 with image2, to capture an image of Planet9 with spectrograph1, to capture an image of Star3 with thermograph0 and to capture an image of Star7 with thermograph0.
