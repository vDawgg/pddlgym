## Problem description

There are five robots (driver1 - driver5), five trucks (truck1 - truck5), twenty packages (package1 - package20) and twenty locations (s0 - s19). The driver driver1 starts out at location s11, driver2 at s6, driver3 at s1, driver4 at s2 and driver5 at s4. The truck truck1 starts out at location s12, truck2 at s3, truck3 at s8, truck4 at s8 and truck5 at s2. The package package1 starts out at location s3, package2 at s18, package3 at s0, package4 at s2, package5 at s3, package6 at s10, package7 at s7, package8 at s7, package9 at s18, package10 at s3, package11 at s16, package12 at s10, package13 at s13, package14 at s14, package15 at s3, package16 at s2, package17 at s18, package18 at s15, package19 at s16 and package20 at s9. The goal is for driver1 to be at s1, driver5 at s9, truck1 at s17, truck2 at s0, truck3 at s3, truck5 at s9, package1 at s1, package2 at s7, package4 at s10, package5 at s15, package6 at s7, package7 at s10, package9 at s7, package10 at s10, package11 at s13, package12 at s12, package13 at s12, package14 at s15, package15 at s18, package16 at s8, package17 at s16, package18 at s3, package19 at s2.
