## Problem description

There are 24 given blocks (b0 - b23). There are 6 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b9-b10-b11-b12, b13-b14-b15-b16, b17-b18-b19, b20-b21-b22-b23, b0-b1-b2-b3, b4-b5-b6-b7-b8. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b4-b7-b6-b8-b11, b1-b0-b19-b13, b2-b5-b17-b14, b9-b15-b20.
