## Problem description

There are two robots (driver1, driver2), two trucks (truck1, truck2), two packages (package1, package2) and three locations (s0 - s2). The driver driver1 starts out at location s2 and driver2 at s2. The truck truck1 starts out at location s0 and truck2 at s0. The package package1 starts out at location s0 and package2 at s0. The goal is for driver1 to be at s1, truck1 at s1, package1 at s0 and package2 at s0.
