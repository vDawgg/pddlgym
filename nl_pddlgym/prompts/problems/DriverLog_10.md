## Problem description

There are two robots (driver1, driver2), three trucks (truck1, truck2, truck3), six packages (package1 - package6) and seven locations (s0 - s6). The driver driver1 starts out at location s6 and driver2 at s0. The truck truck1 starts out at location s0, truck2 at s2 and truck3 at s2. The package package1 starts out at location s6, package2 at s2, package3 at s3, package4 at s0, package5 at s5 and package6 at s5. The goal is for driver2 to be at s4, truck2 at s4, package1 at s6, package2 at s3, package3 at s4, package4 at s6 and package5 at s1.
