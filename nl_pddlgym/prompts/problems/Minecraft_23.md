## Problem description

There are 5 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1, grass-2, grass-3, grass-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-4. grass-0 is at loc-0-0, grass-1 at loc-2-1, grass-2 at loc-1-1, grass-3 at loc-1-4, grass-4 at loc-1-2 and the agent is at loc-2-3. The goal is for the agent to carry grass-0 in its inventory.
