## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), eight cars (c0 - c7), and ten locations (l0 - l9). The ferry start out at location l1. The car c0 is at location l8, c1 at l0, c2 at l9, c3 at l7, c4 at l4, c5 at l1, c6 at l7, and c7 at l0. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l2, c1 to l5, c2 to l7, c3 to l8, c4 to l7, c5 to l8, c6 to l4 and c7 to l9.
