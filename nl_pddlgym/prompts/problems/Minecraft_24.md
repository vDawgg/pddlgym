## Problem description

There are 5 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1, grass-2, grass-3, grass-5), 1 log (log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-4. grass-0 is at loc-1-4, grass-1 at loc-3-1, grass-2 at loc-1-0, grass-3 at loc-3-3, grass-5 at loc-2-4, log-4 at loc-2-0 and the agent is at loc-2-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-3-0 and carry grass-1 in its inventory.
