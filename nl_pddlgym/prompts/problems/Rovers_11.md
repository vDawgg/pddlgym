## Problem description

There are 4 rovers (rover0 - rover3), one lander (general), 4 rover storages (rover0store - rover3store), 4 cameras (camera0 - camera3), 4 objectives (objective0 - objective3), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 8 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint7). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint4, rover1 starts out at location waypoint4, rover2 starts out at location waypoint7 and rover3 starts out at location waypoint7. The lander general starts out at location waypoint2. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The storage rover2store is empty and attached to rover2. The storage rover3store is empty and attached to rover3. The rover rover0 is equipped for imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil and rock analysis. The rover rover2 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped with the camera camera0 and camera3. Camera camera0 can capture high_res mode and has the calibration target objective2 and camera camera3 can capture colour, high_res and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective3. The rover rover2 is equipped with the camera camera1 that can capture the following modes: high_res. The camera camera1 has the calibration target objective1. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera2 that can capture the following modes: low_res. The camera camera2 has the calibration target objective0. waypoint0 contains soil and rock samples and waypoint2, waypoint3, waypoint5, waypoint6 and waypoint7 contain rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint0, rock data from waypoint3, rock data from waypoint6, a high_res image of objective1, a low_res image of objective2 and a low_res image of objective3.
