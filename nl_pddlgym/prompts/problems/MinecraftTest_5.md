## Problem description

There are 4 grass-items (grass-2, grass-3, grass-4, grass-5), 2 logs (log-0, log-1), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-3. grass-2 is at loc-1-1, grass-3 at loc-1-2, grass-4 at loc-0-3, grass-5 at loc-0-3, log-0 at loc-1-3, log-1 at loc-0-2 and the agent is at loc-3-3. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-2-0 and have crafted a plank in new-2.
