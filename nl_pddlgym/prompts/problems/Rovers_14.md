## Problem description

There are 4 rovers (rover0 - rover3), one lander (general), 4 rover storages (rover0store - rover3store), 4 cameras (camera0 - camera3), 5 objectives (objective0 - objective4), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 11 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint10). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint4, rover1 starts out at location waypoint6, rover2 starts out at location waypoint6 and rover3 starts out at location waypoint4. The lander general starts out at location waypoint9. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The storage rover2store is empty and attached to rover2. The storage rover3store is empty and attached to rover3. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for imaging. The rover rover2 is equipped for soil analysis. The rover rover3 is equipped for rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped with the camera camera0 that can capture the following modes: low_res. The camera camera0 has the calibration target objective2. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera1 that can capture the following modes: colour, high_res and low_res. The camera camera1 has the calibration target objective4. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera2 and camera3. Camera camera2 can capture high_res and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective4 and camera camera3 can capture colour, high_res and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective3. waypoint2, waypoint8 and waypoint10 contain soil and rock samples and waypoint0, waypoint4, waypoint5 and waypoint7 contain soil samples and waypoint1 and waypoint9 contain rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint0, soil data from waypoint2, soil data from waypoint5, soil data from waypoint8, soil data from waypoint10, rock data from waypoint1, rock data from waypoint2, rock data from waypoint8, a high_res image of objective1 and a low_res image of objective1.
