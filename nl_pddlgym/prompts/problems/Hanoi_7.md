## Problem description

There are 4 pegs (peg1 - peg4) and 7 rings (d1 - d7). The rings have the following order according to their size (largest to smallest): d7, d6, d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. peg2, peg3 and peg4 have no rings stacked on them. peg1 has rings stacked on it in the following order (bottom to top): d7, d6, d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. The rings should be stacked from largest to smallest on peg4.
