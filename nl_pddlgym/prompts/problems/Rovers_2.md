## Problem description

There are 2 rovers (rover0, rover1), one lander (general), 2 rover storages (rover0store, rover1store), 2 cameras (camera0, camera1), 2 objectives (objective0, objective1), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 4 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint3). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint1 and rover1 starts out at location waypoint3. The lander general starts out at location waypoint0. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera0 that can capture the following modes: low_res. The camera camera0 has the calibration target objective1. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera1 that can capture the following modes: colour, high_res and low_res. The camera camera1 has the calibration target objective0. waypoint2 contains soil and rock samples and waypoint0 and waypoint1 contain rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint2, rock data from waypoint0 and a colour image of objective0.
