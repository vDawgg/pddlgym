## Problem description

There is one needle (at location called: needle) lying on the ground in proximity of the right_arm and one goal position (at location called: goal) close to the left_arm. Additionally, there are 16 rings (at locations called: yellow_ring, orange_ring, purple_ring, pink_ring, cyan_ring, magenta_ring, teal_ring, brown_ring, gray_ring, olive_ring, maroon_ring, navy_ring, lime_ring, coral_ring, gold_ring and silver_ring) in the middle of the scene.

The goal is for the robot to transfer the needle to the goal after passing the needle through the rings in the following sequence:
1. yellow_ring
2. orange_ring
3. purple_ring
4. pink_ring
5. cyan_ring
6. magenta_ring
7. teal_ring
8. brown_ring
9. gray_ring
10. olive_ring
11. maroon_ring
12. navy_ring
13. lime_ring
14. coral_ring
15. gold_ring
16. silver_ring
