## Problem description

There are two planes (plane1, plane2), four passengers (person1, person2, person3, person4), four cities (city0, city1, city2, city3) and 7 possible fuel levels (fl0 - fl6). Plane1 starts out in city1 with fuel level fl6 and plane2 starts out in city2 with fuel level fl0. Passenger person1 is in city3, person2 is in city0, person3 is in city0 and person4 is in city1. The goal is that person1 is in city2, person2 is in city3, person3 is in city3 and person4 is in city3.
