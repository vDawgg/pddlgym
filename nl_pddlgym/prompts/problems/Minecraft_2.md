## Problem description

There are 3 grass-items (grass-0, grass-2, grass-4), 3 log (log-1, log-3, log-5), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-0 is at loc-0-1, grass-2 at loc-0-2, grass-4 at loc-1-1, log-1 at loc-4-2, log-3 at loc-4-0, log-5 at loc-4-1 and the agent is at loc-1-1. The goal is for the agent to have grass-2 equipped and carry log-3 in its inventory.
