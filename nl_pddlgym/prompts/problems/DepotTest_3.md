## Problem description

There are 3 depots (depot0 - depot2), 3 distributors (distibutor0 - distributor2), 2 trucks (truck0, truck1), 10 pallets (palet0 - pallet9), 10 crates (crate0 - crate9) and 6 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist5). pallet0 and pallet9 are at depot0, pallet1 and pallet7 at depot1, pallet2 at depot2, pallet3 at distributor0, pallet4 and pallet8 at distributor1, and pallet5 and pallet6 at distributor2. crate0 is on pallet8, crate1 is on pallet9 with crate5 stacked on top, crate2 is on pallet3 with crate9 stacked on top, crate3 is on pallet6, crate4 is on pallet0, crate6 is on pallet4 with crate7 stacked on top, and crate8 is on pallet1. truck0 is at depot1 and truck1 at depot2. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at depot1, hoist2 at depot2, hoist3 at distributor0, hoist4 at distributor1 and hoist5 at distributor2. The goal is to transfer crate1 on pallet8, crate2 on pallet3, crate4 on pallet0, crate5 on pallet5, crate6 on pallet1, crate7 on crate6 and crate9 on crate7.
