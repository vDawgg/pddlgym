## Problem description

There are five robots (driver1 - driver5), five trucks (truck1 - truck5), ten packages (package1 - package10) and fifteen locations (s0 - s14). The driver driver1 starts out at location s14, driver2 at s1, driver3 at s0, driver4 at s10 and driver5 at s12. The truck truck1 starts out at location s7, truck2 at s1, truck3 at s2, truck4 at s13 and truck5 at s3. The package package1 starts out at location s0, package2 at s14, package3 at s4, package4 at s3, package5 at s11, package6 at s7, package7 at s12, package8 at s8, package9 at s2 and package10 at s7. The goal is for driver1 to be at s12, driver2 at s11, driver3 at s11, driver4 at s12, driver5 at s11, truck1 at s5, truck2 at s6, truck3 at s8, truck4 at s12, truck5 at s14, package1 at s13, package2 at s0, package4 at s11, package5 at s9, package6 at s2, package7 at s3, package8 at s6, package9 at s4 and package10 at s8.
