## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-3), 3 logs (log-0, log-1, log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-3 is at loc-4-0, log-0 at loc-1-1, log-1 at loc-3-1, log-2 at loc-0-2 and the agent is at loc-4-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-1-1.
