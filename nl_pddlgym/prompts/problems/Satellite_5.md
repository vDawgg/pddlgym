## Problem description

There are 3 satellites (satellite0, satellite1, satellite2), 5 instruments (instrument0 - instrument4), 4 different image modes (infrared1, infrared3, spectrograph0 and thermograph2) and 11 directions (GroundStation3, Phenomenon8, Planet4 - Planet5 and Star0, Star1, Star2, Star6, Star7, Star9, Star10). The satellites satellite0, satellite1 and satellite2 have power available. The satellite satellite0 carries the instrument instrument0. The satellite satellite1 carries the instruments instrument1, instrument2 and instrument3. The satellite satellite2 carries the instrument instrument4. The instrument instrument0 supports infrared1 and spectrograph0. The instrument instrument0 is currently calibrated for the direction Star1. The instrument instrument1 supports infrared3. The instrument instrument1 is currently calibrated for the direction Star2. The instrument instrument2 supports infrared1, infrared3 and thermograph2. The instrument instrument2 is currently calibrated for the direction Star2. The instrument instrument3 supports infrared1, infrared3 and spectrograph0. The instrument instrument3 is currently calibrated for the direction Star2. The instrument instrument4 supports infrared3. The instrument instrument4 is currently calibrated for the direction Star0. The satellite satellite0 is currently pointing in the direction of Phenomenon8, satellite1 is currently pointing in the direction of Star6 and satellite2 is currently pointing in the direction of Star6. The goal is to capture an image of Phenomenon8 with spectrograph0, to capture an image of Planet4 with thermograph2, to capture an image of Planet5 with spectrograph0, to capture an image of Star10 with infrared3, to capture an image of Star6 with thermograph2, to capture an image of Star7 with infrared3 and to capture an image of Star9 with infrared1.
