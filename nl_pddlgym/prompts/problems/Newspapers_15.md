## Problem description

There are eighteen locations (loc-0 - loc-17) and twenty-five newspapers (paper-0 - paper-24). The robot starts out at loc-0. All newspapers are located at loc-0. The following locations needs newspapers delivered: loc-1, loc-2, loc-3, loc-4, loc-5, loc-6, loc-7, loc-8, loc-9, loc-10, loc-11, loc-12, loc-13, loc-14, loc-15, loc-16 and loc-17.
