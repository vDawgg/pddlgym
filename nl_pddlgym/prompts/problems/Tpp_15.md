The market market3 has 1 level of goods of type goods2, 2 levels of goods7, 2 levels of goods8, 2 levels of goods9 and 1 level of goods11 for sale. The market market4 has 2 levels of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods3, 2 levels of goods4, 2 levels of goods5, 1 level of goods6, 2 levels of goods7, 1 level of goods8, 1 level of goods9, 2 levels of goods10 and 2 levels of goods11 for sale. No goods have been purchased yet at any market. The truck truck1 is at depot2, truck2 is at depot2, truck3 is at depot2 and truck4 is at depot1. The goal is to store 1 level of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods2, 4 levels of goods3, 4 levels of goods4, 2 levels of goods5, 1 level of goods6, 4 levels of goods7, 2 levels of goods8, 3 levels of goods9, 4 levels of goods10 and 2 levels of goods11 at the depot.
