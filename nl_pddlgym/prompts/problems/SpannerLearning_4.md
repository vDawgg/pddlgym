## Problem description

There is one robot (bob), one spanners (spanner1), one nut (nut1) and three locations (location1, shed, gate). The robot starts out in the shed. The spanner spanner1 is at location location1. The spanner is useable. The nut nut1 is on the gate. The nut is loose. The location shed is connected with the location location1 and location1 is connected with gate. The goal is to tighten the nut nut1.
