## Problem description

There is 1 depot (depot0), 2 distributors (distibutor0, distributor1), 2 trucks (truck0, truck1), 3 pallets (palet0, pallet1, pallet2), 2 crates (crate0, crate1) and 3 hoitsts (hoist0, hoist1, hoist2). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1 at distributor0 and pallet2 at distributor1. crate0 is on pallet1 and crate1 is on pallet0. truck0 is at distributor1, truck1 at depot0 and hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at distributor0 and hoist2 at distributor1. The goal is to transfer crate0 on pallet2 and crate1 on pallet1.
