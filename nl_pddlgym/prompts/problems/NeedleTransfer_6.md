## Problem description

There is one needle (at location called: needle) lying on the ground in proximity of the right_arm and one goal position (at location called: goal) close to the left_arm. Additionally, there are 5 rings (at locations called: magenta_ring, teal_ring, brown_ring, gray_ring and olive_ring) in the middle of the scene.

The goal is for the robot to transfer the needle to the goal after passing the needle through the rings in the following sequence:
1. magenta_ring
2. teal_ring
3. brown_ring
4. gray_ring
5. olive_ring
