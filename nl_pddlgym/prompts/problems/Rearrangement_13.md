## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-2-3 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-1-1. There are two monkeys (monkey-0, monkey-1). The monkey monkey-0 starts out at location loc-1-2. The monkey monkey-1 starts out at location loc-1-1. The goal is to move monkey-1 to loc-1-0.
