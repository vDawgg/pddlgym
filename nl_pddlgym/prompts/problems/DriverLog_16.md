## Problem description

There are five robots (driver1 - driver5), five trucks (truck1 - truck5), fifteen packages (package1 - package15) and sixteen locations (s0 - s15). The driver driver1 starts out at location s10, driver2 at s13, driver3 at s10, driver4 at s9 and driver5 at s2. The truck truck1 starts out at location s2, truck2 at s8, truck3 at s12, truck4 at s3 and truck5 at s10. The package package1 starts out at location s12, package2 at s14, package3 at s3, package4 at s6, package5 at s2, package6 at s1, package7 at s0, package8 at s12, package9 at s12, package10 at s5, package11 at s1, package12 at s9, package13 at s2, package14 at s15 and package15 at s14. The goal is for driver2 to be at s1, driver4 at s1, driver5 at s15, truck1 at s14, truck2 at s1, truck3 at s5, truck4 at s8, truck5 at s6, package1 at s1, package2 at s15, package3 at s10, package4 at s3, package5 at s6, package6 at s8, package7 at s0, package8 at s13, package9 at s1, package10 at s11, package11 at s8, package12 at s6, package13 at s10, package14 at s1 and package15 at s4.
