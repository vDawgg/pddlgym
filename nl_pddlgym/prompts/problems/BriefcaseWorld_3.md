## Problem description

There are five locations (l0 - l4) and four items (o0 - o3). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l3. The item o0 starts out at location l2, o1 at l0, o2 at l3 and o3 at l4. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l2, the item o0 to be at location l4, o1 at l2, o2 at l4 and o3 at l3.
