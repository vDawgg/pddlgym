## Problem description

There are two robots (driver1, driver2), three trucks (truck1, truck2, truck3), six packages (package1 - package6) and six locations (s0 - s5). The driver driver1 starts out at location s4 and driver2 at s0. The truck truck1 starts out at location s0, truck2 at s4 and truck3 at s5. The package package1 starts out at location s1, package2 at s0, package3 at s4, package4 at s4, package5 at s4 and package6 at s2. The goal is for driver1 to be at s1, driver2 at s0, package1 at s5, package2 at s1, package3 at s5, package4 at s1, package5 at s3 and package6 at s2.
