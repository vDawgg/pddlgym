## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-1, grass-3), 4 logs (log-0, log-2, log-4, log-5), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. grass-1 is at loc-1-0, grass-3 at loc-3-0, log-0 at loc-1-0, log-2 at loc-0-0, log-4 at loc-0-1, log-5 at loc-3-0 and the agent is at loc-3-2. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-4-1.
