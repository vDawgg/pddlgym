## Problem description

There are ten locations (l0 - l9) and nine items (o0 - o8). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l0. The item o0 starts out at location l8, o1 at l6, o2 at l0, o3 at l5, o4 at l6, o5 at l9, o6 at l3, o7 at l6 and o8 at l9. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l5, the item o0 to be at location l8, o1 at l5, o2 at l7, o3 at l0, o4 at l1, o5 at l5, o6 at l5, o7 at l0 and o8 at l0.
