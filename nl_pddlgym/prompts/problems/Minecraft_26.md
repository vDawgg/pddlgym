## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-1, grass-3), 2 logs (log-0, log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-3. grass-1 is at loc-1-2, grass-3 at loc-2-3, log-0 at loc-2-0, log-2 at loc-3-1 and the agent is at loc-4-0. The goal is for the agent to have new-0 equipped.
