## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-3-3 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-3-3. There is one monkey (monkey-0), one pawn (pawn-1) and one bear (bear-2). The monkey monkey-0 starts out at location loc-3-2. The pawn pawn-1 starts out at location loc-0-3. The bear bear-2 starts out at location loc-2-2. The goal is for the robot to hold bear-2 while bear-2 is at loc-2-2.
