## Problem description

There are 8 colored pegs with the following colors: brown, cyan, gray, magenta, maroon, olive, purple and teal. The pegs are all named in the format <color>_peg. Additionally there are 6 colored rings: brown, cyan, gray, magenta, purple and teal. Similarly to the pegs, the rings are named <color>_ring.

The robot arm starts out in a default position, while the rings start in the following positions:
- purple_ring - purple_peg
- cyan_ring - cyan_peg
- magenta_ring - teal_peg
- teal_ring - brown_peg
- brown_ring - gray_peg
- gray_ring - magenta_peg

The goal is to transfer all rings to the pegs of their color.
