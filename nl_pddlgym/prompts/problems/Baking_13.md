## Problem description

There are 5 ovens (oven-0 - oven-4), 5 eggs (egg-0 - egg-4), 4 bags of flour (flour-0 - flour-3), one bar of soap (soap-0), 4 clean pans (pan-0 - pan-3), and 4 cakes that should be baked (new-0 - new-3) and 1 souffle that should be baked (new-4). The goal is to bake the cakes new-0 - new-3 and the souffle new-4.
