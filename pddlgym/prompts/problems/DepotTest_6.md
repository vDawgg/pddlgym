## Problem description

There are 2 depots (depot0, depot1), 2 distributors (distibutor0, distributor1), 4 trucks (truck0 - truck3), 8 pallets (palet0 - pallet7), 10 crates (crate0 - crate9) and 8 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist7). pallet0 and pallet6 are at depot0, pallet1 at depot1, pallet2 and pallet4 at distributor0, and pallet3, pallet5 and pallet7 at distributor1. crate0 is on pallet3 with crate5 stacked on top and crate7 on crate5, crate1 is on pallet5, crate2 is on pallet4, crate3 is on pallet6, crate4 is on pallet1, crate6 is on pallet7 with crate8 stacked on top, and crate9 is on pallet2. truck0 and truck2 are at depot1, truck1 at distributor1 and truck3 at depot0. hoist0, hoist4, hoist5 and hoist6 are at depot0, hoist1 at depot1, hoist2 at distributor0, and hoist3 and hoist7 at distributor1. The goal is to transfer crate1 on pallet7, crate2 on pallet4, crate3 on crate8, crate4 on pallet0, crate6 on pallet1, crate7 on crate3 and crate8 on pallet6.
