## Problem description

There are 3 logs (log-0, log-1, log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-2. log-0 is at loc-0-0, log-1 at loc-0-2, log-2 at loc-4-1 and the agent is at loc-2-1. The goal is for the agent to have crafted a plank in new-0.
