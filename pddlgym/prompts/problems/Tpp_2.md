## Problem description

There are 3 types of goods (goods1, goods2, goods3), 1 truck (truck1), 1 market (market1), 1 depot (depot1), and 2 possible levels (level0, level1). The truck has no goods loaded. The depot has no goods stored. The market market1 has 1 level of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods2 and 1 level of goods3 for sale. No goods have been purchased yet at any market. The truck truck1 is at depot1. The goal is to store 1 level of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods2 and 1 level of goods3 at the depot.
