The item o0 starts out at location l13, o1 at l4, o2 at l5, o3 at l5, o4 at l3, o5 at l6, o6 at l9, o7 at l5, o8 at l1, o9 at l13, o10 at l7, o11 at l11 and o12 at l12. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l1, the item o0 to be at location l8, o1 at l11, o2 at l9, o3 at l11, o4 at l2, o5 at l13, o6 at l13, o7 at l10, o8 at l0, o9 at l6, o10 at l0, o11 at l5 and o12 at l0.
