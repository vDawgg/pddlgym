There are 3 depots (depot0 - depot2), 3 distributors (distibutor0 - distributor2), 2 trucks (truck0, truck1), 6 pallets (palet0 - pallet5), 15 crates (crate0 - crate14) and 6 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist5). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1 at depot1, pallet2 at depot2, pallet3 at distributor0, pallet4 at distributor1 and pallet5 at distributor2. crate0 is on pallet5 with crate3 stacked on top, crate6 on crate3, crate7 on crate6, crate8 on crate7, crate9 on crate8 and crate13 on crate9, crate1 is on pallet1 with crate5 stacked on top, crate10 on crate5 and crate12 on crate10, crate2 is on pallet3 with crate4 stacked on top, and crate11 is on pallet4 with crate14 stacked on top. truck0 is at distributor1 and truck1 at depot1. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at depot1, hoist2 at depot2, hoist3 at distributor0, hoist4 at distributor1 and hoist5 at distributor2. The goal is to transfer crate0 on pallet4, crate1 on crate12, crate2 on crate0, crate3 on crate9, crate5 on pallet0, crate6 on crate2, crate9 on pallet2, crate10 on crate13, crate12 on pallet5, crate13 on pallet1 and crate14 on crate10.
