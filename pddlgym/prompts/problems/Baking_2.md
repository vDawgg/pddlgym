## Problem description

There are two ovens (oven-0, oven-1), two eggs (egg-0, egg-1), one bag of flour (flour-0), one depleted bar of soap (soap-0), two clean pans (pan-0, pan-1), one cake that should be baked (new-1), and one souffle that should be baked (new-0). The goal is to bake the cake new-1 and the souffle new-0. Additionally, make sure that both ovens are empty in the end.
