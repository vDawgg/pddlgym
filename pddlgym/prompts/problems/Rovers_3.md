## Problem description

There are 2 rovers (rover0, rover1), one lander (general), 2 rover storages (rover0store, rover1store), 3 cameras (camera0, camera1, camera2), 3 objectives (objective0, objective1, objective2), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 4 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint3). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint3 and rover1 starts out at location waypoint2. The lander general starts out at location waypoint2. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera0 that can capture the following modes: colour and high_res. The camera camera0 has the calibration target objective0. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera1 and camera2. Camera camera1 can capture colour and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective0 and camera camera2 can capture low_res mode and has the calibration target objective1. waypoint3 contains soil and rock samples and waypoint2 contains soil samples and waypoint1 contains rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint3, rock data from waypoint1 and a high_res image of objective0.
