## Problem description

There are 3 grass-items (grass-1 - grass-3), 1 log (log-0), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-4. grass-1 is at loc-2-3, grass-2 at loc-2-3, grass-3 at loc-1-3, log-0 at loc-2-2 and the agent is at loc-2-3. The goal is for the agent to carry grass-3 in its inventory.
