## Problem description

There are 14 given blocks (b0 - b13). There are 4 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b10-b11-b12-b13, b0-b1-b2-b3, b4-b5-b6, b7-b8-b9. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b1-b8-b10, b11-b13-b0-b12, b6-b2-b4-b3, b9-b5-b7.
