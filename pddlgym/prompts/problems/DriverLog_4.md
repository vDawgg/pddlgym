## Problem description

There are three robots (driver1, driver2, driver3), two trucks (truck1, truck2), five packages (package1 - package5) and three locations (s0 - s2). The driver driver1 starts out at location s1, driver2 at s0 and driver3 at s0. The truck truck1 starts out at location s1 and truck2 at s1. The package package1 starts out at location s0, package2 at s0, package3 at s2, package4 at s2 and package5 at s1. The goal is for driver2 to be at s2, truck1 at s2, truck2 at s2, package1 at s1, package2 at s1, package3 at s1, package4 at s0 and package5 at s1.
