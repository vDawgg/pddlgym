## Problem description

There are 3 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1, grass-3), 2 logs (log-2, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-4. grass-0 is at loc-4-4, grass-1 at loc-2-2, grass-3 at loc-3-0, log-2 at loc-2-0, log-4 at loc-0-2 and the agent is at loc-3-1. The goal is for the agent to carry log-2 in its inventory.
