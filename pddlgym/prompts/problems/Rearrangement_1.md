## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-4-2 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-3-2. There are three pawns (pawn-0 - pawn-2) and one bear (bear-0). The bear bear-0 starts out at location loc-1-0. The pawn pawn-0 starts out at location loc-1-0, pawn-1 at loc-1-2 and pawn-2 at loc-1-0. The goal is to move bear-0 to loc-4-2 and for the robot to hold pawn-1.
