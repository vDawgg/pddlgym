## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-4-2 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-1. There are four bears (bear-0 - bear-3). The bear bear-0 starts out at location loc-3-1, bear-1 at loc-2-1, bear-2 at loc-0-0 and bear-3 at loc-1-1. The goal is to move bear-3 to loc-0-1.
