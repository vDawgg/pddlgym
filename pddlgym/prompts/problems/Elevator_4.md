## Problem Description

There are thirteen floors (f0 - f12) and six passengers (p0 - p5). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0,f1,f2,f3,f4,f5,f6,f7,f8,f9,f10,f11. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f7, p1 is at f9, p2 is at f5, p3 is at f10, p4 is at f9 and p5 is at f2. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f10, p1 to f7, p2 to f7, p3 to f0, p4 is at f1 and p5 is at f7.
