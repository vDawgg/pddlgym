## Problem description

There are nine locations (l0 - l8) and eight items (o0 - o7). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l2. The item o0 starts out at location l3, o1 at l6, o2 at l1, o3 at l3, o4 at l8, o5 at l8, o6 at l4 and o7 at l0. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l1, the item o0 to be at location l0, o1 at l6, o2 at l8, o3 at l3, o4 at l4, o5 at l5, o6 at l4 and o7 at l1.
