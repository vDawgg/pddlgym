## Problem description

There are 4 grass-items (grass-0, grass-2, grass-3, grass-4), 1 log (log-1), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-4. grass-0 is at loc-0-3, grass-2 at loc-1-2, grass-3 at loc-2-4, grass-4 at loc-1-3, log-1 at loc-1-3 and the agent is at loc-0-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-0-1 and carry log-1 in its inventory.
