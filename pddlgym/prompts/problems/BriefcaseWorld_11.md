## Problem description

There are thirteen locations (l0 - l12) and twelve items (o0 - o11). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l5. The item o0 starts out at location l1, o1 at l5, o2 at l8, o3 at l12, o4 at l3, o5 at l11, o6 at l6, o7 at l1, o8 at l10, o9 at l7, o10 at l0 and o11 at l6. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l9, the item o0 to be at location l8, o1 at l0, o2 at l1, o3 at l11, o4 at l4, o5 at l4, o6 at l9, o7 at l2, o8 at l3, o9 at l1, o10 at l2 and o11 at l10.
