## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-1), 3 logs (log-0, log-2, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-3. grass-1 is at loc-0-1, log-0 at loc-3-1, log-2 at loc-3-3, log-3 at loc-1-3 and the agent is at loc-1-1. The goal is for the agent to have crafted a plank in new-0.
