## Problem description

There are 2 rovers (rover0, rover1), one lander (general), 2 rover storages (rover0store, rover1store), 3 cameras (camera0, camera1, camera2), 2 objectives (objective0, objective1), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 6 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint5). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint1 and rover1 starts out at location waypoint4. The lander general starts out at location waypoint3. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The rover rover0 is equipped for rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera0 and camera1. Camera camera0 can capture colour and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective0 and camera camera1 can capture colour and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective1. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera2 that can capture the following modes: colour and high_res. The camera camera2 has the calibration target objective0. waypoint2, waypoint3 and waypoint5 contain soil and rock samples and waypoint1 and waypoint4 contain soil samples and waypoint0 contains rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint1, soil data from waypoint2, soil data from waypoint4, soil data from waypoint5, rock data from waypoint0, rock data from waypoint2, rock data from waypoint3, a colour image of objective0, a low_res image of objective0 and a low_res image of objective1.
