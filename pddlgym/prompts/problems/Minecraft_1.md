## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-2), 1 log (log-1), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-4. grass-0 is at loc-3-2, grass-2 at loc-0-0, log-1 at loc-3-3 and the agent is at loc-1-3. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-2-4 and have grass-0 equipped.
