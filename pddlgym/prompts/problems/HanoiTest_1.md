## Problem description

There are 3 pegs (peg1 - peg3) and 8 rings (d1 - d8). The rings have the following order according to their size (largest to smallest): d8,d7,d6,d5,d4,d3,d2,d1. peg2 and peg3 have no rings stacked on them. peg1 has rings stacked on it in the following order (bottom to top): d8,d7,d6,d5,d4,d3,d2,d1. The rings should be stacked from largest to smallest on peg peg3.
