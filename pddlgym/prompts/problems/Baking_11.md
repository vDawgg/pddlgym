## Problem description

There are 4 ovens (oven-0 - oven-3), 4 eggs (egg-0 - egg-3), 3 bags of flour (flour-0, flour-1, flour-2), one bar of soap (soap-0), 3 clean pans (pan-0, pan-1, pan-2), and 3 cakes that should be baked (new-0, new-1 and new-2) and 1 souffle that should be baked (new-3). The goal is to bake the cakes new-0, new-1 and new-2 and the souffle new-3.
