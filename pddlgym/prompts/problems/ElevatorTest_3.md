## Problem Description

There are eighteen floors (f0 - f17) and nine passengers (p0 - p8). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0,f1,f2,f3,f4,f5,f6,f7,f8,f9,f10,f11,f12,f13,f14,f15,f16,f17. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f1, p1 is at f9, p2 is at f5, p3 is at f10, p4 is at f15, p5 is at f14, p6 is at f14, p7 is at f5, and p8 is at f6. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f7, p1 to f7, p2 to f7, p3 to f12, p4 is at f1, p5 is at f13, p6 is at f7, and p7 is at f4 and p8 is at f0.
