## Problem description

There are 8 types of goods (goods1 - goods8), 2 trucks (truck1, truck2), 2 markets (market1, market2), 1 depot (depot1), and 3 possible levels (level0, level1, level2). All trucks have no goods loaded. The depot has no goods stored. The market market1 has 1 level of goods of type goods1, 2 levels of goods2, 1 level of goods3, 2 levels of goods4, 2 levels of goods5, 2 levels of goods6, 2 levels of goods7 and 2 levels of goods8 for sale. The market market2 has 1 level of goods of type goods3 for sale. No goods have been purchased yet at any market. The truck truck1 is at depot1 and truck2 is at depot1. The goal is to store 1 level of goods of type goods1, 2 levels of goods2, 1 level of goods3, 2 levels of goods4, 2 levels of goods5, 1 level of goods6, 2 levels of goods7 and 1 level of goods8 at the depot.
