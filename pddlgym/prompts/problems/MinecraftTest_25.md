## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-2, grass-3), 2 logs (log-0, log-1), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-4. grass-2 is at loc-2-2, grass-3 at loc-0-4, log-0 at loc-2-4, log-1 at loc-1-1 and the agent is at loc-2-2. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-1-2 and have crafted a plank in new-0.
