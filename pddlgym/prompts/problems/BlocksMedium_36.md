## Problem description

There are 26 given blocks (b0 - b25). There are 6 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b10-b11-b12-b13, b14-b15-b16-b17, b18-b19-b20-b21, b22-b23-b24-b25, b0-b1-b2-b3-b4, b5-b6-b7-b8-b9. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b4-b18-b17-b19, b0-b3-b11-b21-b20, b6-b9-b15-b24.
