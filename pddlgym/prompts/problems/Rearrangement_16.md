## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-3-4 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-3-0. There is one bear (bear-0) and one pawn (pawn-1). The bear bear-0 starts out at location loc-2-0. The pawn pawn-1 starts out at location loc-3-0. The goal is to move bear-0 to loc-3-1.
