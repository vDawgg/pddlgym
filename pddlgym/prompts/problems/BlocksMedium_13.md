## Problem description

There are 24 given blocks (b0 - b23). There are 5 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b9-b10-b11-b12-b13, b14-b15-b16-b17-b18, b19-b20-b21-b22-b23, b0-b1-b2-b3-b4, b5-b6-b7-b8. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b1-b23-b9-b14-b0, b6-b13-b17-b20-b16, b15-b3-b11-b8-b18, b10-b4-b2-b19, b5-b21-b12-b7-b22.
