## Problem description

There are 1 grass-item (grass-1), 4 logs (log-0, log-2, log-3, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-2. grass-1 is at loc-1-1, log-0 at loc-2-2, log-2 at loc-0-0, log-3 at loc-2-0, log-4 at loc-0-2 and the agent is at loc-1-2. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-1-1.
