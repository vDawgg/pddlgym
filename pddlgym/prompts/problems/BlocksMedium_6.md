## Problem description

There are 25 given blocks (b0 - b24). There are 6 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b8-b9-b10-b11-b12, b13-b14-b15-b16, b0-b1-b2, b17-b18-b19-b20, b21-b22-b23-b24, b3-b4-b5-b6-b7. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b24-b10-b4-b11, b17-b0-b18-b12, b3-b23-b20-b13, b16-b15-b9-b6-b21, b2-b14-b5.
