## Problem description

There are one plane (plane1), three passengers (person1, person2, person3), three cities (city0, city1, city2) and 7 possible fuel levels (fl0 - fl6). The plane starts out in city0 with fuel level fl2. Passenger person1 is in city2,  person2 is in city1 and person3 is in city2. The goal is that plane1 is in city2, person1 is in city1 and person3 is in city2.
