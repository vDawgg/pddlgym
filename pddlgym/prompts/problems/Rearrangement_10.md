## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-3-4 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-1. There are three monkeys (monkey-0 - monkey-2) and one bear (bear-3). The monkey monkey-0 starts out at location loc-3-1. The monkey monkey-1 starts out at location loc-1-4. The monkey monkey-2 starts out at location loc-3-2. The bear bear-3 starts out at location loc-3-2. The goal is to move bear-3 to loc-0-0 and monkey-2 to loc-2-0.
