## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), six cars (c0 - c5), and eleven locations (l0 - l10). The ferry start out at location l2. The car c0 is at location l1, c1 at l0, c2 at l8, c3 at l6, c4 at l5, and c5 at l5. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l8, c1 to l6, c2 to l8, c3 to l1, c4 to l1 and c5 to l6.
