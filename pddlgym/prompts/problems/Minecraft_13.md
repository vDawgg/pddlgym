## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-3), 3 logs (log-1, log-2, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-2. grass-0 is at loc-1-0, grass-3 at loc-2-0, log-1 at loc-0-0, log-2 at loc-2-2, log-4 at loc-2-2 and the agent is at loc-2-0. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-2-0 and having crafted a plank in new-1.
