## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-2, grass-3), 3 logs (log-0, log-1, log-4), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-3. grass-2 is at loc-2-3, grass-3 at loc-0-2, log-0 at loc-1-1, log-1 at loc-3-0, log-4 at loc-0-3 and the agent is at loc-3-2. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-3-3.
