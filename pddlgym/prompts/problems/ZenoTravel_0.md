## Problem description

There are one plane (plane1), two passengers (person1, person2), three cities (city0, city1, city2) and 7 possible fuel levels (fl0 - fl6). The plane starts out in city0 with fuel level fl1. Passenger person1 is in city0 and person2 is in city2. The goal is that the plane is in city1, person1 is in city0 and person2 is in city2.
