## Problem description

There is one robot (bob), one spanners (spanner1), one nut (nut1) and four locations (location1, location2, shed, gate). The robot starts out in the shed. The spanner spanner1 is at location location1. The spanner is useable. The nut nut1 is on the gate. The nut is loose. The location shed is connected with the location location1, location2 is connected with gate and location1 is connected with location2. The goal is to tighten the nut nut1.
