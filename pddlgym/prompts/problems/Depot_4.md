There is 1 depot (depot0), 2 distributors (distibutor0, distributor1), 2 trucks (truck0, truck1), 3 pallets (palet0 - pallet2), 10 crates (crate0 - crate9) and 3 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist2). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1 is at distributor0 and pallet2 is at distributor1. crate0 is on pallet2, crate1 on pallet0, crate2 on crate0, crate3 is on crate1, crate4 on crate3, crate5 on crate2, crate6 on pallet1, crate7 on crate6, crate8 on crate7 and crate9 on crate5. truck0 is at depot0 and truck1 is at distributor0. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at distributor0 and hoist2 at distributor1. The goal is to transfer crate0 on crate5, crate1 on pallet1, crate2 on crate0, crate3 on pallet2, crate4 on crate6, crate5 on crate4, crate6 on crate9, crate7 on crate1, crate8 on crate3 and crate9 on pallet10.
