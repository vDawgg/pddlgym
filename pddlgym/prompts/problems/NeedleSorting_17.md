## Problem description

There are 18 colored needles (the needle locations are called: blue_needle_1 - blue_needle_3, green_needle_1 - green_needle_3, orange_needle_1 - orange_needle_3, purple_needle_1 - purple_needle_3, red_needle_1 - red_needle_3 and yellow_needle_1 - yellow_needle_3) and 6 goals (blue_goal, green_goal, orange_goal, purple_goal, red_goal and yellow_goal). The robot arm start out in a default position, while the needles are scattered randomly away from the goal positions. The goal is to move all needles to their matching goals.
