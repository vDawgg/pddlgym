## Problem description

There are 5 grass-items (grass-1, grass-2, grass-3, grass-4, grass-5), 1 log (log-0), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-3. grass-1 is at loc-4-1, grass-2 at loc-4-0, grass-3 at loc-1-1, grass-4 at loc-1-2, grass-5 at loc-1-0, log-0 at loc-2-3 and the agent is at loc-2-3. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-2-2 and have crafted a plank in new-0.
