## Problem description

There are twelve locations (l0 - l11) and eleven items (o0 - o10). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l4. The item o0 starts out at location l11, o1 at l1, o2 at l9, o3 at l2, o4 at l6, o5 at l9, o6 at l2, o7 at l10, o8 at l6, o9 at l4 and o10 at l3. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l0, the item o0 to be at location l5, o1 at l1, o2 at l6, o3 at l11, o4 at l10, o5 at l3, o6 at l2, o7 at l4, o8 at l10, o9 at l6 and o10 at l4.
