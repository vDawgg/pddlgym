## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-2-4 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-3. There is one pawn (pawn-0) and one monkey (monkey-1). The pawn pawn-0 starts out at location loc-0-4. The monkey monkey-1 starts out at location loc-2-0. The goal is to move pawn-0 to loc-1-3 and monkey-1 to loc-0-2.
