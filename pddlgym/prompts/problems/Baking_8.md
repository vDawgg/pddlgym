## Problem description

There are 2 ovens (oven-0, oven-1), 3 eggs (egg-0, egg-1, egg-2), 2 bags of flour (flour-0, flour-1), one bar of soap (soap-0), 2 clean pans (pan-0, pan-1), and 2 cakes that should be baked (new-0 and new-1) and 1 souffle that should be baked (new-2). The goal is to bake the cakes new-0 and new-1 and the souffle new-2.
