## Problem Description

There are sixteen floors (f0 - f15) and eight passengers (p0 - p7). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0,f1,f2,f3,f4,f5,f6,f7,f8,f9,f10,f11,f12,f13,f14,f15. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f7, p1 is at f9, p2 is at f1, p3 is at f10, p4 is at f9, p5 is at f10, p6 is at f2, and p7 is at f3. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f6, p1 to f3, p2 to f15, p3 to f12, p4 is at f13, p5 is at f11, p6 is at f11, and p7 is at f6.
