## Problem description

There is one rover (rover0), one moon-lander (general), one rover storage (rover0store), one camera (camera0), two objectives (objective0, objective1), three camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and four locations (waypoint0 - waypoint3). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint3. The lander general starts out at location waypoint0. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera0 that can capture the following modes: colour and high_res. The camera camera0 has the calibration target objective1. The rover rover0 is equipeed for rock and soil analysis. All waypoints contain soil and rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint2, rock data from waypoint3 and a high_res image of objective1.
