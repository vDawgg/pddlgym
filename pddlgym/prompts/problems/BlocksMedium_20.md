## Problem description

There are 21 given blocks (b0 - b20). There are 6 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b10-b11-b12-b13-b14, b15-b16-b17, b0-b1-b2, b18-b19-b20, b3-b4-b5, b6-b7-b8-b9. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b2-b16-b1-b17-b10, b5-b11-b13-b6-b14, b12-b7-b18-b8-b19, b0-b15-b4.
