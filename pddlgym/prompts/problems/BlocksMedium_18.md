## Problem description

There are 15 given blocks (b0 - b14). There are 4 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b9-b10-b11, b12-b13-b14, b0-b1-b2-b3-b4, b5-b6-b7-b8. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b10-b1, b6-b0-b13-b8-b3, b14-b5-b12-b7, b11-b4-b2-b9.
