## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1), 1 log (log-2), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-4. grass-0 is at loc-1-2, grass-1 at loc-1-2, log-2 at loc-0-2 and the agent is at loc-1-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-1-0 and carry new-0 in its inventory.
