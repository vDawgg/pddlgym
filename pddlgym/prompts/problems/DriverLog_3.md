## Problem description

There are three robots (driver1, driver2, driver3), two trucks (truck1, truck2), four packages (package1 - package4) and three locations (s0 - s2). The driver driver1 starts out at location s1, driver2 at s1 and driver3 at s0. The truck truck1 starts out at location s1 and truck2 at s0. The package package1 starts out at location s2, package2 at s2, package3 at s0 and package4 at s1. The goal is for driver1 at s1, driver2 at s0, driver3 at s1, truck1 at s1, truck2 at s2, package1 at s1, package2 at s2, package3 at s2 and package4 at s0.
