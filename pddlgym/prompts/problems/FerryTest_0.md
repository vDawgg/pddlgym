## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), nine cars (c0 - c8), and ten locations (l0 - l9). The ferry start out at location l6. The car c0 is at location l9, c1 at l2, c2 at l1, c3 at l3, c4 at l7, c5 at l1, c6 at l7, c7 at l5, and c8 at l3. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l1, c1 to l7, c2 to l9, c3 to l8, c4 to l0, c5 to l8, c6 to l9, c7 to l8 and c8 to l2.
