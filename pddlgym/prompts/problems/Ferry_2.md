## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), seven cars (c0 - c6), and ten locations (l0 - l9). The ferry start out at location l1. The car c0 is at location l2, c1 at l3, c2 at l8, c3 at l7, c4 at l5, c5 at l2, and c6 at l3. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l8, c1 to l0, c2 to l3, c3 to l5, c4 to l5, c5 to l2 and c6 to l7.
