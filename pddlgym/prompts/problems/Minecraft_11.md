## Problem description

There are 3 grass-items (grass-0, grass-1, grass-4), 2 logs (log-2, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-3-4. grass-0 is at loc-3-3, grass-1 at loc-3-1, grass-4 at loc-3-3, log-2 at loc-1-2, log-3 at loc-3-4 and the agent is at loc-3-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-0-3 and carry new-0 in its inventory.
