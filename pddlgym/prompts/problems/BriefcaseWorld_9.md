## Problem description

There are eleven locations (l0 - l10) and ten items (o0 - o9). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l6. The item o0 starts out at location l10, o1 at l1, o2 at l2, o3 at l0, o4 at l10, o5 at l8, o6 at l1, o7 at l9, o8 at l3 and o9 at l7. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l4, the item o0 to be at location l5, o1 at l1, o2 at l7, o3 at l10, o4 at l7, o5 at l10, o6 at l9, o7 at l1, o8 at l5 and o9 at l5.
