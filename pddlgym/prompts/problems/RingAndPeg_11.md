## Problem description

There are 6 colored pegs with the following colors: cyan, magenta, orange, purple, red and yellow. The pegs are all named in the format <color>_peg. Additionally there are 4 colored rings: cyan, magenta, orange and purple. Similarly to the pegs, the rings are named <color>_ring.

The robot arm starts out in a default position, while the rings start in the following positions:
- orange_ring - orange_peg
- purple_ring - cyan_peg
- cyan_ring - magenta_peg
- magenta_ring - purple_peg

The goal is to transfer all rings to the pegs of their color.
