## Problem description

There are 4 rovers (rover0 - rover3), one lander (general), 4 rover storages (rover0store - rover3store), 5 cameras (camera0 - camera4), 4 objectives (objective0 - objective3), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 10 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint9). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint1, rover1 starts out at location waypoint4, rover2 starts out at location waypoint0 and rover3 starts out at location waypoint2. The lander general starts out at location waypoint7. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The storage rover2store is empty and attached to rover2. The storage rover3store is empty and attached to rover3. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover2 is equipped for imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped for imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped with the camera camera0 that can capture the following modes: colour and low_res. The camera camera0 has the calibration target objective2. The rover rover2 is equipped with the camera camera1 that can capture the following modes: colour. The camera camera1 has the calibration target objective3. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera2 and camera3. Camera camera2 can capture low_res mode and has the calibration target objective3 and camera camera3 can capture colour and low_res mode and has the calibration target objective0. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera4 that can capture the following modes: colour and low_res. The camera camera4 has the calibration target objective3. waypoint3 and waypoint4 contain soil and rock samples and waypoint6 and waypoint9 contain soil samples and waypoint1, waypoint5 and waypoint8 contain rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint3, soil data from waypoint6, rock data from waypoint4, rock data from waypoint5, rock data from waypoint8, a colour image of objective0, a low_res image of objective0 and a low_res image of objective2.
