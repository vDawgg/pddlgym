## Problem description

There are 4 rovers (rover0 - rover3), one lander (general), 4 rover storages (rover0store - rover3store), 4 cameras (camera0 - camera3), 3 objectives (objective0, objective1, objective2), 3 camera modes (colour, high_res, low_res) and 8 locations (waypoint0 - waypoint7). The rover rover0 starts out at location waypoint1, rover1 starts out at location waypoint3, rover2 starts out at location waypoint3 and rover3 starts out at location waypoint7. The lander general starts out at location waypoint1. The storage rover0store is empty and attached to rover0. The storage rover1store is empty and attached to rover1. The storage rover2store is empty and attached to rover2. The storage rover3store is empty and attached to rover3. The rover rover0 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped for soil and rock analysis and imaging. The rover rover2 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover3 is equipped for soil analysis and imaging. The rover rover1 is equipped with the camera camera0 that can capture the following modes: high_res and low_res. The camera camera0 has the calibration target objective1. The rover rover2 is equipped with the camera camera1 that can capture the following modes: colour and high_res. The camera camera1 has the calibration target objective0. The rover rover3 is equipped with the camera camera2 that can capture the following modes: high_res. The camera camera2 has the calibration target objective0. The rover rover0 is equipped with the camera camera3 that can capture the following modes: colour. The camera camera3 has the calibration target objective1. waypoint0, waypoint1 and waypoint6 contain soil and rock samples and waypoint3 contains soil samples and waypoint2, waypoint4 and waypoint7 contain rock samples. The goal is to send soil data from waypoint0, soil data from waypoint1, soil data from waypoint3, soil data from waypoint6, rock data from waypoint0, rock data from waypoint4, rock data from waypoint6, rock data from waypoint7 and a high_res image of objective1.
