## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-1, grass-2), 4 logs (log-0, log-3, log-4, log-5), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-3. grass-1 is at loc-4-0, grass-2 at loc-2-1, log-0 at loc-3-0, log-3 at loc-4-0, log-4 at loc-2-2, log-5 at loc-1-0 and the agent is at loc-3-1. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-0-1.
