## Problem Description

There are 14 floors (f0 - f13) and 15 passengers (p0 - p14). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0, f1, f2, f3, f4, f5, f6, f7, f8, f9, f10, f11, f12, f13. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f0, p1 is at f1, p2 is at f2, p3 is at f3, p4 is at f4, p5 is at f5, p6 is at f6, p7 is at f7, p8 is at f8, p9 is at f9, p10 is at f10, p11 is at f11, p12 is at f12, p13 is at f13 and p14 is at f0. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f1, p1 to f2, p2 to f3, p3 to f4, p4 to f5, p5 to f6, p6 to f7, p7 to f8, p8 to f9, p9 to f10, p10 to f11, p11 to f12, p12 to f13, p13 to f1 and p14 to f2.
