## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-3-3 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-0-0. There is one bear (bear-0), two monkeys (monkey-1, monkey-2) and one pawn (pawn-3). The bear bear-0 starts out at location loc-2-1. The monkey monkey-1 starts out at location loc-1-2. The monkey monkey-2 starts out at location loc-1-1. The pawn pawn-3 starts out at location loc-0-1. The goal is to move monkey-1 to loc-3-3 and monkey-2 to loc-3-2.
