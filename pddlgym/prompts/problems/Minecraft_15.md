## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-4, grass-5), 4 logs (log-0, log-1, log-2, log-3), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-4-4. grass-4 is at loc-3-1, grass-5 at loc-1-0, log-0 at loc-1-3, log-1 at loc-4-3, log-2 at loc-1-1, log-3 at loc-1-1 and the agent is at loc-3-0. The goal is for the agent to have log-3 equipped.
