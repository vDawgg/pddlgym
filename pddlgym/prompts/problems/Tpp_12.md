## Problem description

There are 8 types of goods (goods1 - goods8), 4 trucks (truck1 - truck4), 4 markets (market1 - market4), 2 depots (depot1, depot2), and 4 possible levels (level0 - level3). All trucks have no goods loaded. All depots have no goods stored. The market market1 has 1 level of goods of type goods2, 1 level of goods3 and 1 level of goods5 for sale. The market market2 has 2 levels of goods of type goods1, 2 levels of goods2, 1 level of goods3, 2 levels of goods5, 1 level of goods6 and 2 levels of goods7 for sale. The market market3 has 1 level of goods of type goods3 and 2 levels of goods8 for sale. The market market4 has 1 level of goods of type goods1, 2 levels of goods4, 2 levels of goods6, 1 level of goods7 and 1 level of goods8 for sale. No goods have been purchased yet at any market. The truck truck1 is at depot1, truck2 is at depot1, truck3 is at depot2 and truck4 is at depot2. The goal is to store 2 levels of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods2, 1 level of goods3, 2 levels of goods4, 3 levels of goods5, 1 level of goods6, 1 level of goods7 and 2 levels of goods8 at the depot.
