## Problem description

There are 8 pegs (peg1 - peg8) and 8 rings (d1 - d8). The rings have the following order according to their size (largest to smallest): d8, d7, d6, d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. peg2, peg3, peg4, peg5, peg6, peg7 and peg8 have no rings stacked on them. peg1 has rings stacked on it in the following order (bottom to top): d8, d7, d6, d5, d4, d3, d2, d1. The rings should be stacked from largest to smallest on peg8.
