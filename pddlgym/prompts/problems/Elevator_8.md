## Problem Description

There are 6 floors (f0 - f5) and 5 passengers (p0 - p4). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0, f1, f2, f3, f4, f5. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f0, p1 is at f1, p2 is at f2, p3 is at f3 and p4 is at f4. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f1, p1 to f2, p2 to f3, p3 to f4 and p4 to f5.
