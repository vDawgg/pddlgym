## Problem description

There is one needle (at location called: needle) lying on the ground in proximity of the right_arm and one goal position (at location called: goal) close to the left_arm. Additionally, there are 7 rings (at locations called: yellow_ring, orange_ring, purple_ring, pink_ring, cyan_ring, magenta_ring and teal_ring) in the middle of the scene.

The goal is for the robot to transfer the needle to the goal after passing the needle through the rings in the following sequence:
1. yellow_ring
2. orange_ring
3. purple_ring
4. pink_ring
5. cyan_ring
6. magenta_ring
7. teal_ring
