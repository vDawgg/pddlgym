## Problem description

There are 3 ovens (oven-0, oven-1, oven-2), 3 eggs (egg-0, egg-1, egg-2), 3 bags of flour (flour-0, flour-1, flour-2), one depleted bar of soap (soap-0), 3 clean pans (pan-0, pan-1, pan-2), and 3 cakes that should be baked (new-0, new-1 and new-2). The goal is to bake the cakes new-0, new-1 and new-2.
