## Problem description

There is one robot (bob), two spanners (spanner1, spanner2), two nuts (nut1, nut2) and four locations (location1, location2, shed, gate). The robot starts out in the shed. The spanner spanner1 is at location location1 and spanner2 is at location2. Both spanners are useable. The nut nut1 is on the gate and nut2 is on the gate. Both nuts are loose. The location shed is connected with the location location1, location2 is connected with gate and location1 is connected with location2. The goal is to tighten the nuts nut1 and nut2.
