## Problem description

There are 3 depots (depot0 - depot2), 3 distributors (distibutor0 - distributor2), 2 trucks (truck0, truck1), 6 pallets (palet0 - pallet5), 10 crates (crate0 - crate9) and 6 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist5). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1 at depot1, pallet2 at depot2, pallet3 at distributor0, pallet4 at distributor1 and pallet5 at distributor2. crate0 is on pallet1 with crate3 stacked on top, crate1 is on pallet0, crate2 is on pallet2 with crate4 stacked on top, crate5 on crate4 and crate9 on crate5, and crate6 is on pallet5 with crate7 stacked on top and crate8 on crate7. truck0 is at depot2 and truck1 at distributor0. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at depot1, hoist2 at depot2, hoist3 at distributor0, hoist4 at distributor1 and hoist5 at distributor2. The goal is to transfer crate0 on crate7, crate1 on pallet4, crate2 on pallet5, crate3 on crate9, crate4 on pallet0, crate5 on pallet2, crate6 on crate5, crate7 on crate1, crate8 on pallet3 and crate9 on crate2.
