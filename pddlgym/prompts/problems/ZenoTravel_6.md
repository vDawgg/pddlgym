## Problem description

There are two planes (plane1, plane2), six passengers (person1, person2, person3, person4, person5, person6), four cities (city0, city1, city2, city3) and 7 possible fuel levels (fl0 - fl6). Plane1 starts out in city2 with fuel level fl1 and plane2 starts out in city1 with fuel level fl1. Passenger person1 is in city3, person2 is in city3, person3 is in city3, person4 is in city1, person5 is in city3 and person6 is in city0. The goal is that plane2 is in city1, person1 is in city2, person3 is in city3, person4 is in city3, person5 is in city2 and person6 is in city2.
