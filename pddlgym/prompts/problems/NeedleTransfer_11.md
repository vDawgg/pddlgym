## Problem description

There is one needle (at location called: needle) lying on the ground in proximity of the right_arm and one goal position (at location called: goal) close to the left_arm. Additionally, there are 10 rings (at locations called: blue_ring, yellow_ring, orange_ring, purple_ring, pink_ring, cyan_ring, magenta_ring, teal_ring, brown_ring and gray_ring) in the middle of the scene.

The goal is for the robot to transfer the needle to the goal after passing the needle through the rings in the following sequence:
1. blue_ring
2. yellow_ring
3. orange_ring
4. purple_ring
5. pink_ring
6. cyan_ring
7. magenta_ring
8. teal_ring
9. brown_ring
10. gray_ring
