## Problem Description

There are twenty-one floors (f0 - f20) and ten passengers (p0 - p9). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0,f1,f2,f3,f4,f5,f6,f7,f8,f9,f10,f11,f12,f13,f14,f15,f16,f17,f18,f19. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f3, p1 is at f17, p2 is at f13, p3 is at f6, p4 is at f9, p5 is at f2, p6 is at f10, p7 is at f3, p8 is at f0 and p9 is at f12. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f6, p1 to f15, p2 to f15, p3 to f12, p4 is at f1, p5 is at f7, p6 is at f19, p7 is at f6, p8 is at f6 and p9 is at f16.
