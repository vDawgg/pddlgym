## Problem description

The possible locations are layed out in a grid from loc-0-0 (top-left) to loc-4-2 (bottom-right). The robot starts out at location loc-1-0. There is one monkey (monkey-0), two pawns (pawn-1, pawn-3) and one bear (bear-2). The monkey monkey-0 starts out at location loc-0-0. The pawn pawn-1 starts out at location loc-0-0. The bear bear-2 starts out at location loc-2-0. The pawn pawn-3 starts out at location loc-0-1. The goal is to move pawn-1 to loc-3-1 and bear-2 to loc-3-2.
