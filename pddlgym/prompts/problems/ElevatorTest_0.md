## Problem Description

There are fourteen floors (f0 - f13) and seven passengers (p0 - p6). The floors are arranged in the following order (bottom to top): f0,f1,f2,f3,f4,f5,f6,f7,f8,f9,f10,f11,f12,f13. The lift is currently at floor f0. The passenger p0 is at floor f1, p1 is at f9, p2 is at f1, p3 is at f10, p4 is at f1, p5 is at f2, p6 is at f6. The goal is to transfer the passenger p0 to floor f4, p1 to f5, p2 to f3, p3 to f2, p4 is at f3, p5 is at f5, and p6 is at f11.
