## Problem description

There are 6 colored pegs with the following colors: brown, gray, maroon, navy, olive and red. The pegs are all named in the format <color>_peg. Additionally there are 5 colored rings: brown, gray, maroon, navy and olive. Similarly to the pegs, the rings are named <color>_ring.

The robot arm starts out in a default position, while the rings start in the following positions:
- brown_ring - brown_peg
- gray_ring - olive_peg
- olive_ring - maroon_peg
- maroon_ring - navy_peg
- navy_ring - gray_peg

The goal is to transfer all rings to the pegs of their color.
