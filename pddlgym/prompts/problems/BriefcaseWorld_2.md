## Problem description

There are four locations (l0 - l3) and three items (o0, o1, o2). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l0. The item o0 starts out at location l0, o1 at l1 and o2 at l1. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l1, the item o0 to be at location l0, o1 at l0 and o2 at l2.
