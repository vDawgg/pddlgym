## Problem description

There are 2 depots (depot0, depot1), 2 distributors (distibutor0, distributor1), 4 trucks (truck0 - truck3), 8 pallets (palet0 - pallet7), 6 crates (crate0 - crate5) and 8 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist7). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1, pallet4 and pallet6 at depot1, pallet2 and pallet7 at distributor0, and pallet3 and pallet5 at distributor1. crate0 is on pallet4, crate1 is on pallet1 with crate3 stacked on top, crate2 is on pallet0 with crate5 stacked on top, and crate4 is on pallet2. truck0 and truck1 are at depot1, truck2 at depot0 and truck3 at distributor1. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1, hoist5 and hoist6 at depot1, hoist2 at distributor0, and hoist3, hoist4 and hoist7 at distributor1. The goal is to transfer crate0 on pallet3, crate2 on pallet1, crate3 on pallet0, crate4 on crate3 and crate5 on pallet2.
