## Problem description

There are seven locations (l0 - l6) and six items (o0 - o5). The robot with the briefcase starts out at location l3. The item o0 starts out at location l0, o1 at l0, o2 at l2, o3 at l4, o4 at l5 and o5 at l3. The goal is for the robot with the briefcase to be at location l3, the item o0 to be at location l1, o1 at l4, o2 at l6, o3 at l4, o4 at l5 and o5 at l0.
