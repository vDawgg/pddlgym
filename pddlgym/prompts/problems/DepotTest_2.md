## Problem description

There are 3 depots (depot0 - depot2), 3 distributors (distibutor0 - distributor2), 2 trucks (truck0, truck1), 10 pallets (palet0 - pallet9), 6 crates (crate0 - crate5) and 6 hoitsts (hoist0 - hoist5). pallet0 is at depot0, pallet1 at depot1, pallet2 at depot2, pallet3 and pallet9 at distributor0, pallet4 and pallet6 at distributor1, pallet5 at distributor2, and pallet7 and pallet8 at depot0. crate0 is on pallet2 with crate1 stacked on top and crate5 on crate1, crate2 is on pallet0, crate3 is on pallet8 and crate4 is on pallet3. truck0 is at distributor1 and truck1 at depot0. hoist0 is at depot0, hoist1 at depot1, hoist2 at depot2, hoist3 at distributor0, hoist4 at distributor1 and hoist5 at distributor2. The goal is to transfer crate0 on pallet0, crate1 on pallet5, crate2 on pallet4, crate3 on pallet7, crate4 on pallet9 and crate5 on pallet1.
