## Problem description

There are 3 ovens (oven-0, oven-1, oven-2), 4 eggs (egg-0 - egg-3), 2 bags of flour (flour-0, flour-1), 2 bars of soap (soap-0, soap-1), 2 clean pans (pan-0, pan-1), and 2 cakes that should be baked (new-0 and new-1) and 2 souffles that should be baked (new-2 and new-3). The goal is to bake the cakes new-0 and new-1 and the souffles new-2 and new-3.
