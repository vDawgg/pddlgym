## Problem description

There are two planes (plane1, plane2), five passengers (person1, person2, person3, person4, person5), three cities (city0, city1, city2) and 7 possible fuel levels (fl0 - fl6). Plane1 starts out in city2 with fuel level fl5 and plane2 starts out in city2 with fuel level fl0. Passenger person1 is in city0, person2 is in city1, person3 is in city0, person4 is in city0 and person5 is in city2. The goal is that plane1 is in city0, person2 is in city2, person3 is in city0, person4 is in city1 and person5 is in city2.
