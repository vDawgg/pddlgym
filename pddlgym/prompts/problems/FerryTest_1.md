## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), five cars (c0 - c4), and eleven locations (l0 - l10). The ferry start out at location l4. The car c0 is at location l10, c1 at l3, c2 at l9, c3 at l1, and c4 at l8. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l4, c1 to l3, c2 to l8, c3 to l3 and c4 to l2.
