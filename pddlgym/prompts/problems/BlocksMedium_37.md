## Problem description

There are 28 given blocks (b0 - b27). There are 6 stacks of blocks on the table (given in the order from top to bottom) b10-b11-b12-b13-b14, b15-b16-b17-b18-b19, b20-b21-b22-b23, b24-b25-b26-b27, b0-b1-b2-b3-b4, b5-b6-b7-b8-b9. The goal is to rearrange the blocks in the following stacks: b18-b17-b23-b13, b9-b19-b22-b6-b21, b7-b10-b5-b16-b26.
