## Problem description

There are 2 grass-items (grass-1, grass-2), 1 log (log-0), 3 crafting-slots (new-0 - new-2) and one agent. The available locations are arranged in a grid and named loc-0-0 to loc-2-4. grass-1 is at loc-0-4, grass-2 at loc-2-3, log-0 at loc-1-4 and the agent is at loc-2-2. The goal is for the agent to move to loc-1-4.
