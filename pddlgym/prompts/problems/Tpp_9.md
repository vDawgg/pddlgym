## Problem description

There are 10 types of goods (goods1 - goods10), 3 trucks (truck1, truck2, truck3), 3 markets (market1, market2, market3), 1 depot (depot1), and 3 possible levels (level0, level1, level2). All trucks have no goods loaded. The depot has no goods stored. The market market1 has 1 level of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods3, 1 level of goods4, 2 levels of goods5 and 2 levels of goods7 for sale. The market market2 has 1 level of goods of type goods1, 2 levels of goods2, 1 level of goods3, 1 level of goods4, 1 level of goods6, 2 levels of goods8, 1 level of goods9 and 1 level of goods10 for sale. The market market3 has 1 level of goods of type goods10 for sale. No goods have been purchased yet at any market. The truck truck1 is at depot1, truck2 is at depot1 and truck3 is at depot1. The goal is to store 2 levels of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods2, 2 levels of goods3, 2 levels of goods4, 2 levels of goods5, 1 level of goods6, 2 levels of goods7, 2 levels of goods8, 1 level of goods9 and 1 level of goods10 at the depot.
