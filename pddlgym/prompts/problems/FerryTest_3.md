## Problem description

There is one ferry (ferry), seven cars (c0 - c6), and eleven locations (l0 - l10). The ferry start out at location l10. The car c0 is at location l5, c1 at l10, c2 at l6, c3 at l6, c4 at l3, c5 at l0, and c6 at l4. The goal is to transfer car c0 to location l4, c1 to l10, c2 to l0, c3 to l5, c4 to l8, c5 to l9 and c6 to l9.
