## Problem description

There are 5 types of goods (goods1 - goods5), 2 trucks (truck1, truck2), 2 markets (market1, market2), 1 depot (depot1), and 2 possible levels (level0, level1). All trucks have no goods loaded. The depot has no goods stored. The market market1 has 1 level of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods2, 1 level of goods3, 1 level of goods4 and 1 level of goods5 for sale. No goods have been purchased yet at any market. The truck truck1 is at depot1 and truck2 is at depot1. The goal is to store 1 level of goods of type goods1, 1 level of goods2, 1 level of goods3, 1 level of goods4 and 1 level of goods5 at the depot.
