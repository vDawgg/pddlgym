## Problem description

There are 10 colored needles (the needle locations are called: blue_needle_1 - blue_needle_4, green_needle_1 - green_needle_3 and red_needle_1 - red_needle_3) and 3 goals (blue_goal, green_goal and red_goal). The robot arm start out in a default position, while the needles are scattered randomly away from the goal positions. The goal is to move all needles to their matching goals.
