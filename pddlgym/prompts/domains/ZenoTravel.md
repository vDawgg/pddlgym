## Domain description

A robot is tasked with flying a set of passangers from one city to another.

The actions available to the robot are:
- board
    - Boards a passenger on the plane at the current location.
- debark
    - Debarks a passenger from the plane at the current location.
- fly
    - Flys a passenger from one location to another while consuming one level of fuel.
- refuel
    - Refuels the plane to the next-highest fuel level at the current location.
