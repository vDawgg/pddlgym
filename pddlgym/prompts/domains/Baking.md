## Domain description

A robot is tasked with baking a recipe with the avilable ingredients. Note that the pan should be clean before baking and that a bar of soap is depleted once used for cleaning, meaning it can no longer be used.

The actions available to the robot are:
- putegginpan
    - Puts a egg in a pan
- putflourinpan
    - Puts flour in a pan
- mix
    - Mixes together an egg and flour in a pan
- putpaninoven
    - Puts a pan in an oven
- removepanfromoven
    - Removes a pan from an oven
- bakecake
    - Bakes a cake
- bakesouffle
    - Bakes a souffle
- cleanpan
    - Cleans a pan
